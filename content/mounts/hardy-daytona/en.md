**Hardy-Daytona** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: Hardy-Daytona.

Call into battle your Hardy-Daytona, a lore-bending battlebike that will have you feeling as though you were playing a minigame.
