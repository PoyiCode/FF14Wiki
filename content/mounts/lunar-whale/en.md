title: "Lunar Whale"
summary: "A mount for getting around."
---

**Lunar Whale** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: Lunar Whale.

Summon forth the Lunar Whale, which is definitely not an actual whale, but certainly large enough to warrant the name. Seats up to eight people, including the pilot.
