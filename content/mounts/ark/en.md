title: "Ark"
summary: "A mount for getting around."
---

**Ark** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: Ark.

Summon forth your Ark, a bipedal warmachina disguised as an airship—or an airship disguised as a bipedal warmachina, if you prefer.
