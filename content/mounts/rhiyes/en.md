**Rhiyes** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: Rhiyes.

Summon forth your Rhiyes, a vibrantly hued mechanical rhinoceros that can't say no to terrorizing the Beans of Blunderville.
