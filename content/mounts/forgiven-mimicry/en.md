**forgiven mimicry** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: forgiven mimicry.

Summon forth your forgiven mimicry. Not actually a sin eater, so it can be ridden without fear of turning those around you into one.
