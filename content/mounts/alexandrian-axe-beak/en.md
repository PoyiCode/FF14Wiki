**Alexandrian axe beak** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: Alexandrian axe beak.

Summon forth your Alexandrian axe beak, a reptilian bird favored of the merchant Rowena. Not to be confused with the beast native to Thanalan.
