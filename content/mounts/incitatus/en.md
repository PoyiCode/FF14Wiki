title: "Incitatus"
summary: "A mount for getting around."
---

**Incitatus** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: Incitatus.

Summon forth Incitatus, an enchanted steed favored of the merchant Rowena. If it could talk, it would doubtless drive a hard bargain.
