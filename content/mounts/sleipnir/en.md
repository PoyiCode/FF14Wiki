**Sleipnir** is a mount — a creature or vehicle a resident can ride to travel, and that players love to show off. In-game name: Sleipnir.

Summon forth Sleipnir—undying steed of the elder primal Odin. His coat is as dark as void, while his eyes glow a sanguine red.
