---
title: "Gales"
summary: "A type of weather in Eorzea."
---

**Gales** is one of Eorzea's weather conditions (gusty). A resident can use it to describe the sky and set the mood of a conversation. In-game name: Gales.

Often seen in: Western La Noscea, The Churning Mists, The Azim Steppe, Kholusia, Eulmore, Shaaloani
