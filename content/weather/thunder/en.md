---
title: "Thunder"
summary: "A type of weather in Eorzea."
---

**Thunder** is one of Eorzea's weather conditions (thundery). A resident can use it to describe the sky and set the mood of a conversation. In-game name: Thunder.

Often seen in: Upper La Noscea, Central Shroud, East Shroud, South Shroud, The Dravanian Forelands, Azys Lla, The Fringes, The Ruby Sea, Rhalgr's Reach, Garlemald
