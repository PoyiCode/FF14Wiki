---
title: "Showers"
summary: "A type of weather in Eorzea."
---

**Showers** is one of Eorzea's weather conditions (showery). A resident can use it to describe the sky and set the mood of a conversation. In-game name: Showers.

Often seen in: Eastern La Noscea, Eastern Thanalan, North Shroud, The Dravanian Hinterlands, Idyllshire, Yanxia, The House of the Fierce, The Doman Enclave, Thavnair, Kozama'uka
