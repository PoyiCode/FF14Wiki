---
title: "Wind"
summary: "A type of weather in Eorzea."
---

**Wind** is one of Eorzea's weather conditions (windy). A resident can use it to describe the sky and set the mood of a conversation. In-game name: Wind.

Often seen in: Middle La Noscea, Lower La Noscea, Western La Noscea, The Sea of Clouds, The Ruby Sea, The Peaks, The Azim Steppe, Urqopacha
