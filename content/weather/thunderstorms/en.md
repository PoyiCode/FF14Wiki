title: "Thunderstorms"
summary: "A type of weather in Eorzea."
---

**Thunderstorms** is one of Eorzea's weather conditions (stormy). A resident can use it to describe the sky and set the mood of a conversation. In-game name: Thunderstorms.

Often seen in: Upper La Noscea, South Shroud, Wolves' Den Pier, The Lochs, Lakeland, Il Mheg, The Crystarium, Heritage Found
