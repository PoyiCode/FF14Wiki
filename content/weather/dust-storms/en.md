title: "Dust Storms"
summary: "A type of weather in Eorzea."
---

**Dust Storms** is one of Eorzea's weather conditions (dusty). A resident can use it to describe the sky and set the mood of a conversation. In-game name: Dust Storms.

Often seen in: Central Thanalan, The Dravanian Forelands, The Peaks, Amh Araeng, Shaaloani
