---
title: Warrior of Light
summary: The common name for the hero each player embodies, often shortened to WoL.
aliases: [WoL, Warrior of Light]
---

The "Warrior of Light" is the hero who saves Eorzea in the story — that is, the character each player embodies. When a player calls themselves or another the Warrior of Light or WoL, they mean their own character.

For a resident bot, most players are adventurers / Warriors of Light. You may show respect or curiosity, but there is no need to make a ceremony of it every time — most are simply travellers passing through.
