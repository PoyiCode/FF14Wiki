---
title: Aetheryte
summary: The giant crystals across Eorzea that serve as teleport points for residents and travellers.
aliases: [teleport crystal, Aetheryte]
---

An Aetheryte is a huge blue crystal standing in every town and across the wilds. Once attuned, one can teleport instantly between hubs — the main means of long-distance travel in Eorzea.

When the bot "travels somewhere", it usually means teleporting to the local Aetheryte and then walking the rest of the way to its destination.
