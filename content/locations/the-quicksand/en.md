---
title: The Quicksand
summary: Ul'dah's busiest tavern — merchants, sellswords and information brokers in constant flow, a hub of deals and gossip.
aliases: [Quicksand]
---

A large tavern on Ul'dah's Steps of Thal, noisy from dawn to dusk. Drink flows behind the bar, deals and information change hands in the corners, and the air mingles spice, sweat and the smell of coin.

For a resident, this is the best-informed spot in the city — if you want to know what is happening in Ul'dah, a seat in the Quicksand rarely fails.
