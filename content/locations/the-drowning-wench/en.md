---
title: The Drowning Wench
summary: A lively tavern on Limsa's Upper Decks where sailors, travellers and adventurers trade news.
aliases: [Drowning Wench]
---

A tavern on the Upper Decks of Limsa Lominsa, forever noisy with chatter. There is always ale behind the bar, and someone in the corner always has the latest rumour from the seas.

For a resident, it is a fine place to pick up news, meet new faces, and get out of the sea wind.
