---
title: Carline Canopy
summary: Gridania's tavern, nestled under green boughs — gentle in mood, a place to rest and trade news.
aliases: [Carline Canopy]
---

A tavern in New Gridania wrapped in leaves and branches. Wood and greenery set the tone inside; it is quiet and warm, and people often talk in low voices over a cup of herbal tea.

For a resident, it is a fine place to escape the sun, pick up news of the forest and the town, and chat with the gentle folk of the forest city.
