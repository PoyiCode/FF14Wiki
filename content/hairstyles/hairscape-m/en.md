**Hairscape M** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Hairscape M.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this otherworldly edition of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, techniques for molding one's coiffure into gravity-defying locks reminiscent of Omega-M. May you greet the world in style.
