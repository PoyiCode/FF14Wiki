**Tail of the Teller** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Tail of the Teller.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this specific copy of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, techniques for a ponytail that suggests a great destiny awaits the wearer, in spite of its simplicity.
