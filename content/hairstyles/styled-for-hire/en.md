title: "Styled for Hire"
summary: "A hairstyle."
---

**Styled for Hire** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Styled for Hire.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this specific copy of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, techniques for trimming and coiffing one's way to the dashing look that's in vogue amongst sellswords across the Three Great Continents.
