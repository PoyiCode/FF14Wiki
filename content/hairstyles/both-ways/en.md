title: "Both Ways"

**Both Ways** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Both Ways.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this specific copy of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, a hair styling technique which marries a braid and a side ponytail in an exquisite union.
