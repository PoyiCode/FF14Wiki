title: "The Warden"
summary: "A hairstyle."
---

**The Warden** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: The Warden.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this legendary edition of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, the cascading coiffure required to emulate the goddess Azeyma Herself.
