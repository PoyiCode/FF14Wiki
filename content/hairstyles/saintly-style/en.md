title: "Saintly Style"
summary: "A hairstyle."
---

**Saintly Style** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Saintly Style.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this limited edition of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, shaping and feathering techniques for imitating the *unique* look of an Ishgardian saint of eld.
