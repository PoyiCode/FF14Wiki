**Ambitious Ends** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Ambitious Ends.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this specific copy of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, styling techniques used to mixed success since the time of the ancients.
