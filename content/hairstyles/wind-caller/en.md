**Wind Caller** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Wind Caller.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this limited edition of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, feathering and styling techniques to achieve the windswept look of a legendary Vartan warrior.
