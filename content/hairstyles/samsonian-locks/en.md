**Samsonian Locks** is a hairstyle a resident can wear — a way to change one's look and show off some personal style. In-game name: Samsonian Locks.

The realm's premier publication on beauty and fashion, this specific copy of *Modern Aesthetics* covers, in detail, methods for styling longer hair.
