title: "Yellow Gatherers' Scrip"
summary: "A currency of Eorzea."
---

**Yellow Gatherers' Scrip** is a currency or token used in Eorzea — something a resident earns and spends. In-game name: Yellow Gatherers' Scrip.

Currency paid to Disciples of the Land by Rowena's House of Splendors in exchange for services rendered. Can be used to purchase items from the aforementioned establishment. Not valid anywhere else on Hydaelyn.
