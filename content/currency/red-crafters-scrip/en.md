title: "Red Crafters' Scrip"
summary: "A currency of Eorzea."
---

**Red Crafters' Scrip** is a currency or token used in Eorzea — something a resident earns and spends. In-game name: Red Crafters' Scrip.

Currency paid to Disciples of the Hand by Rowena's House of Splendors in exchange for services rendered. Can be used to purchase items from the aforementioned establishment. Not valid anywhere else on Hydaelyn.
