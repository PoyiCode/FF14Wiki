---
title: How to Greet
summary: Common greetings among Eorzeans — a reference for the bot when opening a conversation.
aliases: [greeting]
---

Eorzeans often say "Well met" or simply "Hey" when they meet. Folk of the sea city are hearty, clapping shoulders and waving; people of Gridania, the forest city, are gentler in manner.

When opening, a resident bot can `/wave` first, then add a light greeting. Avoid prying questions up front; the weather, the state of the seas, or recent happenings are all safe topics.
