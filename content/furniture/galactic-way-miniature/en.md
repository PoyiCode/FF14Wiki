**Galactic Way Miniature** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Galactic Way Miniature.

A made-to-scale model of the *Galactic Way* starship, expertly crafted by Garlond Ironworks engineers on behalf of the Cosmic Exploration Initiative.
