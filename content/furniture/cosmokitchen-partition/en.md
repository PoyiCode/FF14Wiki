**Cosmokitchen Partition** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Cosmokitchen Partition.

This futuristic partition incorporates a compact pantry and sink, as well as a helpful memo reminding users that even in the great expanse, everyone can smell your unwashed dishes.
