**Deluxe Oriental Wind Chime** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Deluxe Oriental Wind Chime.

This small glass bell has a slip of parchment attached to its clapper which, when blown by the wind, causes the clapper to strike the rim. Completely different than last year's chime, we promise.
