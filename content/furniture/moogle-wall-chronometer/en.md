**Moogle Wall Chronometer** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Moogle Wall Chronometer.

When faced with the dilemma of adorning his wall with a normal chronometer or a chronometer carved in the adorable likeness of a moogle, it is said the dread (though most likely nonexistent) pirate Mustbeard replied with a hearty “arrr” and found room for both.
