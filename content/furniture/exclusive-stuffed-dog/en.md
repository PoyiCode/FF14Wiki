**Exclusive Stuffed Dog** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Exclusive Stuffed Dog.

Said to bring a full twelvemoon of good fortune...or as much can be expected from a disturbingly realistic dog skin filled with sawdust and bittersweet memories. Perhaps this is related to why there are a limited number in circulation.
