**Pumpkin Pastry Platter** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Pumpkin Pastry Platter.

In the spirit of All Saints' Wake, each of these pastries is pumpkin-flavored...except for the pumpkin-shaped one, which was deemed thematically sufficient regardless. Also in the spirit of All Saints' Wake, there are more of them than is advisable to consume in one sitting.
