**Evercold Shaved Ice** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Evercold Shaved Ice.

Thinly shaved ice, generously heaped into an oversmall bowl and drizzled with sweet-yet-tangy rolanberry syrup. Why it never melts is a question best served cold.
