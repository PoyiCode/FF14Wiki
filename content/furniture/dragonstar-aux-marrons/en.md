**Dragonstar aux Marrons** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Dragonstar aux Marrons.

An ode to the Dragonstar, in dessert form. Fittingly, perhaps, this edible delight is a reimagining of the Sohm Al tart.
