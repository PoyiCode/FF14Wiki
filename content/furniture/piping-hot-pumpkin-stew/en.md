**Piping Hot Pumpkin Stew** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Piping Hot Pumpkin Stew.

An All Saints' Wake culinary tradition, this rich, buttery soup is oft served in a hollowed-out pumpkin. This year's batch is undeniably hotter than last year's.
