**Valentione's Day Balloons** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Valentione's Day Balloons.

To create these festive balloons, the bladders of various flying fish caught in the Sea of Clouds were dried, colored, then stamped with the House Valentione coat of arms before being filled with heated air to keep them afloat.
