---
title: "Kugane-yaki"
summary: "A piece of furniture."
---

**Kugane-yaki** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Kugane-yaki.

The official name of this Far Eastern snack is hotly contested, with some insisting upon “blade buns” as a nod to the fan-like pattern traditionally stamped on top with a branding iron. Have care when ordering them at a restaurant, lest you inadvertently start an argument.
