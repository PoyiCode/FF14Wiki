**Tier 3 Metal Aquarium** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Tier 3 Metal Aquarium.

A large reinforced glass tank that, when filled with water, can serve as a home for select wavekin caught in the wild. The higher an aquarium's tier, the larger the specimens that can be kept within.
