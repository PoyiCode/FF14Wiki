**Fat Cat Bank** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Fat Cat Bank.

Presumably due to the resemblance in terms of rotundity, the fat cat has overtaken the pig as a popular motif for coin banks. Comes with a matching purse to better facilitate the spending of one's hard-earned savings.
