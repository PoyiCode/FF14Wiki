**Unmoving Maneki Moogle** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Unmoving Maneki Moogle.

This charming knickknack is designed after the maneki neko, a Far Eastern cat fetish whose uplifted paw is believed to beckon fortune to its owner's establishment.
