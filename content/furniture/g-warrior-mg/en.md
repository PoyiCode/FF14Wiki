**G-Warrior MG** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: G-Warrior MG.

A dynamic tabletop statue based on Garlond Ironworks' G-Warrior. The aethersaber emits sufficient light to be used as a bedside reading lamp, but be warned that touching it may result in severed appendages. The “MG” part of its name supposedly stands for “Master Garlond,” or something along those lines.
