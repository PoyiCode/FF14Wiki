**Jumbo Hard Rice Cakes** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Hard Rice Cakes.

Known in the Far East as “kagami mochi,” these cakes are not traditionally eaten, but displayed before and during Heavensturn. The difference in size with normal hard rice cakes is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
