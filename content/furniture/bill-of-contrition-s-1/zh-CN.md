**Bill of Contrition (S-1)** 是居民用来布置房屋的家具摆设。游戏内名称：Bill of Contrition (S-1)。

An illustrated apology issued in lieu of a receipt for a first-class plot of small size. Purchased with gil unintentionally refunded by lottery processing mishaps.
