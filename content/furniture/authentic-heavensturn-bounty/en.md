**Authentic Heavensturn Bounty** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Heavensturn Bounty.

A traditional Heavensturn decoration meant to beckon fortune through its depiction of a traditional bumper crop of rice, traditionally infested by mice. In addition to the stuffed mouse included, it also attracts living mice, which some may find to be a nuisance, but it nevertheless adds to the overall authenticity of the piece.
