**Stuffed Tsukuyomi** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Stuffed Tsukuyomi.

Like the real Tsukuyomi, she looks as if she needs a hug. Unlike the real Tsukuyomi, she will not slice you in half if you try.
