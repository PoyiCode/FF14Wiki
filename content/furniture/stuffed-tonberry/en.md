**Stuffed Tonberry** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Stuffed Tonberry.

This plush doll is rumored to be stuffed with countless strips of cloth covered in terrible curses. Slicing open the tonberry to find out what they entail is not recommended.
