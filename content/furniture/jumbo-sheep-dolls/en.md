**Jumbo Sheep Dolls** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Sheep Dolls.

Two stuffed sheep—husband and wife—dressed in traditional Far Eastern garb. The difference in size with normal sheep dolls is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
