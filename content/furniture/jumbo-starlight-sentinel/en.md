**Jumbo Starlight Sentinel** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Starlight Sentinel.

A potted conifer displayed in homes during the Starlight Celebration. The difference in size with a normal Starlight sentinel is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
