**Necrologos Transcription** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Necrologos Transcription.

While appearing to be gibberish, the words written within this tome fiendishly incorporate a summoning spell. Readers are advised to exercise caution lest they inadvertently unleash an army of voidsent.
