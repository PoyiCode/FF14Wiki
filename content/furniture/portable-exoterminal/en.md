**Portable Exoterminal** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Portable Exoterminal.

Developed by the Cosmic Exploration Initiative, this device was designed for the purpose of easily sending mass updates to exotablets whether at camp or in the field—though some Loporrits have been caught red-pawed using it to design frivolous games.
