**Savage Gordian Chair** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Savage Gordian Chair.

A chair crafted from gears found within the right arm of the mechanical fortress Alexander. Magicks imbued within the furnishing have been known to stimulate the backsides of those who sit on it for any extended period of time.
