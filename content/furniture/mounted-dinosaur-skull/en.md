**Mounted Dinosaur Skull** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Mounted Dinosaur Skull.

In a case of the hunter becoming the hunted, the cranium of this ferocious predator is now doomed to spend eternity in ignominy mounted as a trophy on an adventurer's wall. Its headless body must be rolling in its grave.
