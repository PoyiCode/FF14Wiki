**Authentic Copy of Under the Moonlight** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Copy of Under the Moonlight.

A masterfully rendered oil depiction of the erstwhile acting imperial viceroy of Doma, Yotsuyu, and those instrumental in her destruction. The depiction of these pivotal figures under the moonlight, the serious moonlight, is unmistakably authentic.
