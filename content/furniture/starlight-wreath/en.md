**Starlight Wreath** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Starlight Wreath.

A simple circle of evergreen branches hung on doors during the Starlight Celebration. While the tradition is fairly new, there already exist several theories as to how the practice started. Some believe the circle represents the symbolic beginning and end of a year, and in turn, our lives, while others claim it is merely easier to hang a ring than it is a straight branch.
