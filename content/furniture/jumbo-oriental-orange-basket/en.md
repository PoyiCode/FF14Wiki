**Jumbo Oriental Orange Basket** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Oriental Orange Basket.

Oriental oranges, while small, are far softer and sweeter than the oranges found growing in Eorzea. The difference in size with a normal oriental orange basket is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
