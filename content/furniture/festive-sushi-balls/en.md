**Festive Sushi Balls** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Festive Sushi Balls.

Off put by the oblongitudinal proportions of regular sushi? Well, your prayers have been answered. All the fresh fish and vinegared rice you love, ham-fisted into semi-sophisticated spheroids perfect for popping.
