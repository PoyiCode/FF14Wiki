**Adventuring Pack** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Adventuring Pack.

An adventurer never leaves home without his compass! And his waterskin. And his lantern. And his potions. And his telescope. And his books. Really, an adventurer needs to learn to pack a bit lighter.
