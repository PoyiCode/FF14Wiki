**Jumbo Paissa Doll** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Paissa Doll.

A stuffed toy recently adopted for use in Heavensturn festivities. The difference in size with a normal paissa doll is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
