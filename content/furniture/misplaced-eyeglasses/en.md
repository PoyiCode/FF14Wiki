**Misplaced Eyeglasses** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Misplaced Eyeglasses.

As the person to whom these eyeglasses belonged is now without eyeglasses, he will likely never be able to find them again and is probably wandering around bumping into things at this very moment.
