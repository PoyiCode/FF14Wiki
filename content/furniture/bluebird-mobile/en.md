**Bluebird Mobile** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Bluebird Mobile.

If one bluebird brings happiness, surely those who adorn their homes with this revolving display have naught to fear—aside from taking a faux bluebird to the eye should they move carelessly.
