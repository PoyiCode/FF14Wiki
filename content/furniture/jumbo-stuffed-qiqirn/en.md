**Jumbo Stuffed Qiqirn** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Stuffed Qiqirn.

A handmade doll crafted to resemble a Qiqirn, and dressed in a festive Starlight Celebration outfit. The difference in size with a normal stuffed Qiqirn is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
