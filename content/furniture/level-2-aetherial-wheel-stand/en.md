**Level 2 Aetherial Wheel Stand** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Level 2 Aetherial Wheel Stand.

A metal stand upon which aetherial wheels are placed and spun to charge. Holds one grade 1 and one grade 2 wheel.
