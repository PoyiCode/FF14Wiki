**Fluffy-wuffy Stuffed Drippy** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Fluffy-wuffy Stuffed Drippy.

Crafted to resemble a splishy-wishy young nixie, and infused with levitation magicks, this whimsical plushie floats like a balloon.
