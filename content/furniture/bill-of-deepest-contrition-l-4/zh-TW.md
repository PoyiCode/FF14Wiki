**Bill of Deepest Contrition (L-4)** 是居民用來布置房屋的家具擺設。遊戲內名稱：Bill of Deepest Contrition (L-4)。

An illustrated apology issued in lieu of a receipt for a fourth-class plot of large size. Purchased with gil unintentionally refunded by lottery processing mishaps.
