**Authentic Little Ladies' Day Banner** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Little Ladies' Day Banner.

A princess needs no crown when this banner exists to always remind them how special they are. The feelings of undeniable specialness are—according to the makers, at least—rather authentic.
