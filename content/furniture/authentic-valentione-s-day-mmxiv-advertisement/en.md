**Authentic Valentione's Day MMXIV Advertisement** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Valentione's Day MMXIV Advertisement.

An authentic reproduction of an official Valentione's Day announcement. Without proper advertisement, some may mistake Valentione's Day for any other day, albeit one with far more pink balloons.
