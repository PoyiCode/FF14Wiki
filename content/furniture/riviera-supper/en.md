**Riviera Supper** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Riviera Supper.

While for most La Nosceans supper consists of huddling around a fire pit while sopping up a splash of rancid fish stew with a hunk of moldy bread, those who have found their fortune on the seas prefer a multiple-course extravaganza certain to leave even the most wealthy Ul'dahn speechless.
