**Pumpkin Party Pennants** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Pumpkin Party Pennants.

A festive wall decoration dominated by All Saints' Wake motifs. If one did not know better than to suspect the troupe of trickery, one might think this “reward” merely a hastily assembled hodgepodge of spare party decorations.
