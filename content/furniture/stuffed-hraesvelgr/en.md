**Stuffed Hraesvelgr** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Stuffed Hraesvelgr.

As few homeowners have the space to accommodate a member of the first brood, this stuffed version of Hraesvelgr has been created especially for those who enjoy the sensation of an ancient dragon observing their every move.
