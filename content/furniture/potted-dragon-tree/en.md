**Potted Dragon Tree** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Potted Dragon Tree.

A pruned and potted dragon tree. It is said that anyone in the presence of this breed of flora is unable to tell a lie, though it might also be that anyone in the presence of a dragon tree is unable to ask the hard questions.
