**Bill of Deep Contrition (M-3)** 是居民用来布置房屋的家具摆设。游戏内名称：Bill of Deep Contrition (M-3)。

An illustrated apology issued in lieu of a receipt for a third-class plot of medium size. Purchased with gil unintentionally refunded by lottery processing mishaps.
