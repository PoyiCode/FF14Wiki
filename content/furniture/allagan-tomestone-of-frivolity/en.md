**Allagan Tomestone of Frivolity** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Allagan Tomestone of Frivolity.

While this particular tomestone does not appear to have any function whatsoever, it does add a dash of Allagan style to your interior. Be careful where you place it, however, as it will be even more useless if you accidentally step on it.
