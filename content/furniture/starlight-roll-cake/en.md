**Starlight Roll Cake** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Starlight Roll Cake.

Two cream-filled sponge cakes complete with strawberries on top. While it may not be the most obvious choice for a festive treat, the color scheme certainly conjures up images of the Starlight Celebration.
