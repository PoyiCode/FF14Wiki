**Level 5 Aetherial Wheel Stand** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Level 5 Aetherial Wheel Stand.

A metal stand upon which aetherial wheels are placed and spun to charge. Holds two grade 1, two grade 2, and one grade 3 wheel.
