**Fat Cat Wall Chronometer** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Fat Cat Wall Chronometer.

This convenient timekeeping device will always remind you when it is time to eat—unless, of course, it is time for your pets to eat, in which case you will be repeatedly reminded long before you have the chance to consult your timepiece.
