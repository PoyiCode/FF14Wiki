**Jumbo Starlight Ornament** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Starlight Ornament.

A wall decoration hung in homes during the Starlight Celebration. The difference in size with a normal Starlight ornament is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
