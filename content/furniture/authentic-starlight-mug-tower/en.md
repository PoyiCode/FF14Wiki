**Authentic Starlight Mug Tower** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Starlight Mug Tower.

The best part of drinking kinderpunsch is that after you've had a few, you can stack the mugs to form this authentically festive Starlight decoration.
