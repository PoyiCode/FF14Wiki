**Mannequin** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Mannequin.

Once the nightmare of shopkeeps alone, highly detailed models such as this one have gained popularity amongst adventurers as means of selling unneeded accoutrements, displaying hard-won ensembles, and unnerving unsuspecting visitors.
