**Authentic Rising Balloon** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Rising Balloon.

The bladder of some poor beast, colored with dye and filled with lighter-than-air gas to dazzle the young *and* the young at heart come the Rising. So authentic to the previous year's balloons that you can almost smell it.
