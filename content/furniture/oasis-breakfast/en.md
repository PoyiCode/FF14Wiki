**Oasis Breakfast** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Oasis Breakfast.

Considered the most important of the day's seven meals (preceding both pre-midday mead and eleventh bell tarts), the Ul'dahn elite rarely miss this opportunity to gorge themselves before moving on to the next opportunity to gorge themselves.
