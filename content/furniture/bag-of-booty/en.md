**Bag of Booty** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Bag of Booty.

The shine of the historic coins contained within has been professionally restored with great care. Nevertheless, whether you tuck this bag safely away or wave it about while putting on a terrible faux-pirate accent is entirely up to you.
