**Futon Mattress** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Futon Mattress.

Common in the Far East, futons are thin down-filled mattresses laid directly on the floor instead of upon a frame. After use, they are generally rolled up and stored in a closet until needed again.
