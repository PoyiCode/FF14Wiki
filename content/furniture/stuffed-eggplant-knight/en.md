**Stuffed Eggplant Knight** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Stuffed Eggplant Knight.

Designed with the most complicated pattern of the plush Mandragoras, this knight will protect your castle by day, and woo the queen by night. (Mandragora Queen sold separately.)
