**Helldiver SD** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Helldiver SD.

An amazingly lifelike model constructed using specially engineered cloth and cotton bolls by Ironworks employees with a particular...enthusiasm for the helldiver variety of machina. Designation: snuggly.
