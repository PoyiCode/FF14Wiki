**Pumpkin Pie Set** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Pumpkin Pie Set.

Puréed pumpkin mixed with cream, eggs, and spices, and baked into a flaky, buttery crust. An All Saints' Wake staple (for reasons unknown).
