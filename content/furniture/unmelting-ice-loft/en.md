**Unmelting Ice Loft** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Unmelting Ice Loft.

Though the ability to defy the laws of gravity and nature is certainly impressive, the best thing about this handy platform is that footprints can be made in the thin layer of snow on top. Go ahead, try it!
