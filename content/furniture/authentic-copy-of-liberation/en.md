**Authentic Copy of Liberation** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Copy of Liberation.

A masterfully rendered oil depiction of key players in the conflict between the Garlean Empire and its erstwhile territories Ala Mhigo and Doma. The sense of impending liberation is authentic indeed.
