**Authentic Starlight Celebration MMXVIII Advertisement** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Authentic Starlight Celebration MMXVIII Advertisement.

A commissioned painting which depicts the Starlight Celebration choir and their delighted audience. It is so authentic that snow was used instead of white paint, although this soon melted, revealing the white canvas below.
