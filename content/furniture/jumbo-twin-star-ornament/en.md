**Jumbo Twin Star Ornament** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Jumbo Twin Star Ornament.

A brilliant wall decoration hung in homes during the Starlight Celebration. The difference in size with a normal twin star ornament is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
