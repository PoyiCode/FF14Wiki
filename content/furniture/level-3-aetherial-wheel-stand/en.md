**Level 3 Aetherial Wheel Stand** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Level 3 Aetherial Wheel Stand.

A metal stand upon which aetherial wheels are placed and spun to charge. Holds one grade 1, one grade 2, and one grade 3 wheel.
