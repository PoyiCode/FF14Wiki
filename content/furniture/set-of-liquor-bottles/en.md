**Set of Liquor Bottles** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Set of Liquor Bottles.

Having a comprehensive selection of unopened drinks is a sign of good taste and restraint. These seven bottles of varying colors and sizes will add a touch of class wherever you place them, except for beside your bed, perhaps.
