**Throne of the Foremost** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Throne of the Foremost.

This artifact recovered from the Fell Court of Troia has the distinct air of a furnishing designed for only the greatest of individuals, but technically nothing can stop you from sitting on it.
