**Unfriendly Yan Neon Wall Light** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Unfriendly Yan Neon Wall Light.

A vivid wall light designed in the likeness of an otherworldly—and if its stern countenance is any indication, highly belligerent—sheep spirit.
