**Misplaced Mog Slippers** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Misplaced Mog Slippers.

These delightful slippers can be placed—or misplaced—almost anywhere inside your home, allowing you to enjoy gazing at a prize pair of pom-poms whenever you please.
