**Scattered Documents** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Scattered Documents.

This pile of parchment appears to be pages taken from *The Last Paean*—a detailed account of the life and times of a legendary bard who traveled the realm bringing joy to all he met. Strewn across one's bureau, the documents give any library or study a heightened air of sophistication.
