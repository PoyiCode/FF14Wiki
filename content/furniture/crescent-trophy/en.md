**Crescent Trophy** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Crescent Trophy.

Though it may be difficult to explain how this elegantly sculpted trophy proves that you have visited Shades' Triangle and lived to tell of it, it will come in useful should any naysayers doubt the veracity of your claims to know the names of basic shapes.
