**Kweh-kweh Clock** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Kweh-kweh Clock.

A charming chronometer designed to alert you of the time with a rousing “Kweh-kweh!” at convenient intervals. Recommended to those plagued by unwanted fits of napping.
