**Boar's Head Kadomatsu** is a furnishing residents place to decorate their homes. In-game name: Boar's Head Kadomatsu.

A Heavensturn decoration displayed at homes and places of business. The spinning umbrella is thought to bring fortune as long as an object remains atop.
