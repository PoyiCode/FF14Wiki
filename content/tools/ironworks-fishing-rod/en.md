**Ironworks Fishing Rod** is a tool of the **Fisher**'s trade — an implement a resident plies their craft with. In-game name: Ironworks Fishing Rod.

Rumor has that fishing enthusiast Biggs used the Garlond Ironworks crafting facilities after-hours to create this magitek-enhanced rod.
