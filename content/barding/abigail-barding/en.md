**Abigail Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Abigail Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to fulfill your lifelong desire to see a chocobo dressed like a scullery maid.
