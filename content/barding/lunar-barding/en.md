title: "Lunar Barding"

---

**Lunar Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Lunar Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to resemble Tsukuyomi, Lady of the Moon.
