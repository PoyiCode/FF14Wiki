**Starlight Stalls Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Starlight Stalls Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed and crafted by the Saint of Nymeia himself in order to help spread warmth and cheer at the Starlight Stalls.
