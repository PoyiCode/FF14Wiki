title: "Seiryu Barding"
summary: "Chocobo barding."
---

**Seiryu Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Seiryu Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to resemble the Far Eastern auspice Seiryu.
