**Black Mage Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Black Mage Barding.

An expertly crafted suit of aether-infused chocobo armor, designed to mimic the garb of the black mages of days past.
