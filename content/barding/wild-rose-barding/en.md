**Wild Rose Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Wild Rose Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to resemble that worn by a long-forgotten hero of legend.
