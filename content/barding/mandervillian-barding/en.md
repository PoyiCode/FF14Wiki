**Mandervillian Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Mandervillian Barding.

Expertly tailored trumpery designed to resemble the uniforms worn by the maidservants employed at the Manderville manor.
