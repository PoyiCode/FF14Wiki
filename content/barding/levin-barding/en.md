summary: "Chocobo barding."
---

**Levin Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Levin Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to resemble Ramuh, the Lord of Levin.
