**Flamecloaked Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Flamecloaked Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to resemble Rubicante, the archfiend of fire.
