---

**Orthodox Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Orthodox Barding.

A beautifully ornamented suit of plate armor worn by chocobos in the service of church officiants.
