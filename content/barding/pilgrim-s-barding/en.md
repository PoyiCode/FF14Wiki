title: "Pilgrim's Barding"
summary: "Chocobo barding."
---

**Pilgrim's Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Pilgrim's Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor inspired by designs from the Church of the First Light.
