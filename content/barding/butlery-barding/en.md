title: "Butlery Barding"
summary: "Chocobo barding."
---

**Butlery Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Butlery Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to fool the world into believing your chocobo is a man hired to answer your front door.
