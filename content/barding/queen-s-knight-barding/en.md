**Queen's Knight Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Queen's Knight Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed in the image of a dedicated servant to her sovereign.
