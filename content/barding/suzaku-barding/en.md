---
title: "Suzaku Barding"
summary: "Chocobo barding."
---

**Suzaku Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Suzaku Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to resemble the Far Eastern auspice Suzaku.
