**Wolf Barding** is barding — saddle or armour a resident dresses their chocobo companion in, to ride in style or show off their trusty bird. In-game name: Wolf Barding.

A suit of chocobo armor designed to obfuscate your steed's unfortunate affliction during the full moon.
