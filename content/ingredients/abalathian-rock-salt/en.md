title: "Abalathian Rock Salt"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Abalathian Rock Salt** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Abalathian Rock Salt.

Mined from veins found in Abalathia's Spine, this pinkish salt has a high mineral content which adds depth to its flavor.
