---
title: "Splendid Turmeric"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Splendid Turmeric** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Splendid Turmeric.

Through the work of masters in the ways of spice, this root of decidedly unremarkable appearance is transformed into a bright-orange powder. This would make for a rather impressive magic trick, were it not for the fact the process takes several days to complete, which is somewhat longer than the attention span of the average audience.
