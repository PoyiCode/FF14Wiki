---
title: "Wild Popoto"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Wild Popoto** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Wild Popoto.

Allowed to grow unchecked in the wild, it only took a matter of generations for this transplant from the New World to become decidedly different than its farm-grown cousins.
