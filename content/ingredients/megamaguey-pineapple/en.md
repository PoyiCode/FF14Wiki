---
title: "Megamaguey Pineapple"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Megamaguey Pineapple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Megamaguey Pineapple.

Harvested from the body of a megamaguey. Like agave, it can be used to produce mezcal with a rich and full-bodied flavor.
