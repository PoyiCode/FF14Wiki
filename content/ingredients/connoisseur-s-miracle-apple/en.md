title: "Connoisseur's Miracle Apple"

---

**Connoisseur's Miracle Apple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Connoisseur's Miracle Apple.

A fruit harvested with the discerning standards of an avid collector in mind, and well suited to aetherial reduction (or consumption by hungry artisans).
