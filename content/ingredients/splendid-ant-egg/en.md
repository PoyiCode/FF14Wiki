title: "Splendid Ant Egg"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Splendid Ant Egg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Splendid Ant Egg.

Savory-sweet and nourishing, this ant egg will have any Qitari exclaiming “Splendid!”
