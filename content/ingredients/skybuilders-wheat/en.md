**Skybuilders' Wheat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Skybuilders' Wheat.

Were the other contributors to hear that you traveled to Amh Araeng in the First simply to procure wheat, they would think you are just trying to show off.
