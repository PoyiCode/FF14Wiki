---
title: "Oddly Specific Aloe"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Oddly Specific Aloe** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Oddly Specific Aloe.

Should only be harvested after being lightly dusted by the scales of an endangered species of moth, which also has its own set of oddly specific requirements.
