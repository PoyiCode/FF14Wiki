**Grade 4 Artisanal Skybuilders' Raspberry** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Grade 4 Artisanal Skybuilders' Raspberry.

Being rather small, it is unusual for raspberries to be handled individually rather than by weight. This may also be because fruiterers are keen to avoid counting anything other than coins.
