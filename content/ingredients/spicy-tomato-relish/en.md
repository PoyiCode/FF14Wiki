**Spicy Tomato Relish** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Spicy Tomato Relish.

A zesty Near Eastern relish made by stewing tomatoes with select spices and seasoning.
