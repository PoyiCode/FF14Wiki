title: "Connoisseur's Cloves"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Connoisseur's Cloves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Connoisseur's Cloves.

A material harvested with the discerning standards of an avid collector in mind, and well suited to aetherial reduction. Also, pies.
