**Ripe Steppe Fruit** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ripe Steppe Fruit.

This fruit's bright-orange color speaks to its ripeness...if it is indeed the type of fruit that turns orange when ripe.
