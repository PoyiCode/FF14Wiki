**Approved Grade 2 Skybuilders' Umbral Earthcap** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Approved Grade 2 Skybuilders' Umbral Earthcap.

Charged with aether drawn from the earth. In actual fact, most mushrooms possess some earth-aspected aether, but this particular species is especially greedy.
