title: "Gyr Abanian Wheat"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Gyr Abanian Wheat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gyr Abanian Wheat.

A close cousin to highland wheat, Gyr Abanian wheat has been improved by Ala Mhigan farmers over generations to increase both flavor and yield.
