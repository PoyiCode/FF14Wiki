title: "Approved Grade 2 Skybuilders' Wheat"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Approved Grade 2 Skybuilders' Wheat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Approved Grade 2 Skybuilders' Wheat.

There is a possibility that some grade 1 wheat slipped in by mistake.
