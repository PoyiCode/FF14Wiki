**Prime Kukuru Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Prime Kukuru Beans.

This bean is born of a kukuru tree cultivated within Labyrinthos's precisely controlled climate. Under the right conditions, it could become sublime.
