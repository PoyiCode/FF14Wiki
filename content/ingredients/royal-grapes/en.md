---
title: "Royal Grapes"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Royal Grapes** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Royal Grapes.

The flavor of these cultivated grapes has been intensified using a special bacteria. Though unpleasant to eat whole, they are suitable for juicing and fermentation.
