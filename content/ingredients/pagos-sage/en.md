---
title: "Pagos Sage"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Pagos Sage** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Pagos Sage.

A fragrant herb that can be used medicinally or to add a pleasant flavor to cuisine. The variety found in Pagos is notably crisp and hearty.
