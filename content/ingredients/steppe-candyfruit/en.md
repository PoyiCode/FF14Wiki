title: "Steppe Candyfruit"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Steppe Candyfruit** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Steppe Candyfruit.

Do not let its unappealing exterior fool you—this steppe delicacy is worth the effort of finding one.
