title: "Ja Tiika Cranberries"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Ja Tiika Cranberries** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ja Tiika Cranberries.

The fruit of a shrub which grows only in central Ja Tiika. Unsuited for general consumption, the bitter juice of the berries is boiled and mixed into vitality tonics.
