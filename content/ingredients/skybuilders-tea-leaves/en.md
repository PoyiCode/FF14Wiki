title: "Skybuilders' Tea Leaves"

---

**Skybuilders' Tea Leaves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Skybuilders' Tea Leaves.

The thought of happy skybuilders drinking delicious tea was the only motivation you needed as you risked life and limb gathering these leaves in the Sea of Clouds.
