**Thavnairian Perilla Leaf** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Thavnairian Perilla Leaf.

An annual herb, grown on the island of Thavnair and known for its superb medicinal properties.
