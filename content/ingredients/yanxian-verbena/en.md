title: "Yanxian Verbena"

**Yanxian Verbena** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Yanxian Verbena.

A hearty plant that can proliferate on nearly any surface, including tree trunks. Its leaves release a pleasant scent when touched.
