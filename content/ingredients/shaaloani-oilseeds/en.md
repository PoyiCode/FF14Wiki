---
title: "Shaaloani Oilseeds"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Shaaloani Oilseeds** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Shaaloani Oilseeds.

Indigenous to the wilds of Shaaloani, these robust seeds are developed over a period of months, then processed into oil for cooking.
