**Connoisseur's Glimshroom** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Connoisseur's Glimshroom.

A material harvested with the discerning standards of an avid collector in mind, and well suited to aetherial reduction. Contains all the luminous convenience of a torch without the fire.
