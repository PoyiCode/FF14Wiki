title: "Grade 3 Skybuilders' Tea Leaves"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Grade 3 Skybuilders' Tea Leaves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Grade 3 Skybuilders' Tea Leaves.

This variety is often used in tea leaf reading, and is said to result in a high rate of good fortunes, though most tasseomancers base their readings more on what they think the customer wants to hear than the patterns left in the cup.
