title: "Thavnairian Corn"

**Thavnairian Corn** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Thavnairian Corn.

A selectively bred variant cultivated from the maize once brought to Thavnair from the New World. Valued for its nutritional value and alchemical uses alike.
