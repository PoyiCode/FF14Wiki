---
title: Browse the Market
summary: Wander the city's markets and stalls, eye the wares, and chat with merchants — the most lived-in of resident pastimes.
aliases: [shopping, browse the market]
---

Wind through the markets of Ul'dah or the sea city, taking in the heaped wares, the smell of spice, and a little haggling and small talk with the stallholders. You needn't buy a thing — just soaking up the bustle is its own pleasure.

The bot can stroll among the stalls, remark on curious goods, or steer a passing player toward a good stall. A natural way to blend into city life and open a conversation.
