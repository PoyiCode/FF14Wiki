**Llymlaen, the Navigator** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of wind and watches over the 5th moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Llymlaen, watcher of the seas and goddess of navigation, is the guardian deity of Limsa Lominsa.
She commands the element of wind and is associated with the fifth moon of the Eorzean calendar. Llymlaen is the daughter of Thaliak, and the elder sister of Nophica.
She is most often depicted as a strong fisherwoman wielding a long-bladed harpoon. Her symbol is the wave.
