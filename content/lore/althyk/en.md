title: "Althyk, the Keeper"
summary: "One of the Twelve — guardian deity of the 12th moon."
---

**Althyk, the Keeper** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of earth and watches over the 12th moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Althyk is the surveyor of change and space and god of time.
He commands the element of earth and is associated with the twelfth moon of the Eorzean calendar. Althyk is the father of Azeyma and Menphina, and elder brother to Nymeia.
He is most often depicted as an austere emperor wielding a mythril greataxe. His symbol is the hourglass.
