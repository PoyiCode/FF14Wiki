**Nald'thal, the Traders** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of fire and watches over the 10th moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Nald'thal, overseer of the underworld and god of commerce, is the guardian deity of Ul'dah.
He commands the element of fire and is associated with the tenth moon of the Eorzean calendar. Nald'thal is the single manifestation of the deific twins Nald and Thal.
He is most often depicted as a discerning merchant holding a balance. His symbol is the cowry, an ancient shell currency.
