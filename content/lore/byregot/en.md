---
title: "Byregot, the Builder"
summary: "One of the Twelve — guardian deity of the 7th moon."
---

**Byregot, the Builder** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of lightning and watches over the 7th moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Byregot is the purveyor of architecture and industry, and god of the arts.
He commands the element of lightning and is associated with the seventh moon of the Eorzean calendar. Byregot is the elder brother of Halone, and pupil of Thaliak.
He is most often depicted as an ardent smith with a two-headed hammer. His symbol is the hand.
