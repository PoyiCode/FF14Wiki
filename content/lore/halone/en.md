**Halone, the Fury** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of ice and watches over the 1st moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Halone, mover of glaciers and goddess of war, is the guardian deity of Ishgard.
She commands the element of ice and is associated with the first moon of the Eorzean calendar. Halone is the daughter of Rhalgr, and a bitter rival of Nophica.
She is most often depicted as a relentless warrioress armed with a bronze greatshield. Her symbol is the three spears.
