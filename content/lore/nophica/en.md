title: "Nophica, the Matron"
summary: "One of the Twelve — guardian deity of the 11th moon."
---

**Nophica, the Matron** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of earth and watches over the 11th moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Nophica, tender of soils and harvests and goddess of abundance, is the guardian deity of Gridania.
She commands the element of earth and is associated with the eleventh moon of the Eorzean calendar. Nophica is the daughter of Azeyma, and the younger sister of Llymlaen.
She is most often depicted as a jubilant farmer holding a scythe of steel. Her symbol is the spring leaf.
