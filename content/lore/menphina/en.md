---
title: "Menphina, the Lover"
summary: "One of the Twelve — guardian deity of the 2nd moon."
---

**Menphina, the Lover** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of ice and watches over the 2nd moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Menphina is keeper of the twin moons and the goddess of love.
She commands the element of ice and is associated with the second moon of the Eorzean calendar. Menphina is the sister of Azeyma, and the divine lover of Oschon.
She is most often depicted as a maid carrying a round skillet. Her symbol is the full moon.
