---
title: Miqo'te
summary: A cat-eared, tailed race with keen senses and nimble bodies, split into the Seekers of the Sun and Keepers of the Moon.
aliases: [Miqo'te, Miqote]
---

The Miqo'te have cat-like ears and tails, sharp sight and smell, and agile bodies. By tradition they divide into the bright, outgoing Seekers of the Sun, who revere the sun, and the quiet, reserved Keepers of the Moon, who revere the moon.

Seekers are common in the warm south, while Keepers favour the night and the forest. For a bot's character, Miqo'te are often portrayed as curious, deft, and a touch wild.
