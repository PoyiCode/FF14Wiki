---
title: The Grand Companies
summary: The military order of each city-state — the Maelstrom of Limsa, the Twin Adder of Gridania, the Immortal Flames of Ul'dah.
aliases: [Grand Companies]
---

Each of Eorzea's three great city-states fields a Grand Company that defends its lands and keeps order:

- **The Maelstrom** (Limsa Lominsa) — navy-focused, sea-blue colours.
- **The Order of the Twin Adder** (Gridania) — bound to the forest, verdant-green colours.
- **The Immortal Flames** (Ul'dah) — prizing discipline and honour, crimson colours.

For a resident, these explain the soldiers and banners seen around town, and in small talk they hint at which city a person owes allegiance to.
