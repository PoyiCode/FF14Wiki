**Rhalgr, the Destroyer** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of lightning and watches over the 8th moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Rhalgr, breaker of worlds, is the god of destruction and guardian deity of the now fallen nation of Ala Mhigo.
He commands the element of lightning and is associated with the eighth moon of the Eorzean calendar. Rhalgr is the father of both Byregot and Halone, and serves as attendant to Nymeia.
He is most often depicted as a magus carrying a staff of bronze. His symbol is the streaking meteor.
