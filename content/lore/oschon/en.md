title: "Oschon, the Wanderer"
summary: "One of the Twelve — guardian deity of the 6th moon."
---

**Oschon, the Wanderer** is one of the Twelve, the gods of Eorzea. This deity commands the element of wind and watches over the 6th moon of the Eorzean calendar.

Oschon is ruler of the mountains and god of wanderers and vagrants.
He commands the element of wind and is associated with the sixth moon of the Eorzean calendar. Oschon is the brother of Nald'thal, and the close companion of Halone.
He is most often depicted as a carefree ranger wielding a bow of yew. His symbol is the walking stick.
