---
title: The Twelve
summary: The twelve guardian deities most Eorzeans revere, tied to the stars, the seasons and every facet of life.
aliases: [The Twelve]
---

"The Twelve" are the most widespread faith in Eorzea. Each deity watches over a different facet of nature and life — the sea, the forest, commerce, love, death and more — and corresponds to a month and constellation in the calendar.

Everyday Eorzean speech is laced with the Twelve — "By the Twelve!" in surprise, "Twelve keep you" in parting. For a bot, invoking a god's name at the right moment lends a conversation a more authentically Eorzean flavour.
