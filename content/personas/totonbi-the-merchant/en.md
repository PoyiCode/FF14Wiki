---
title: Totonbi the Merchant
summary: A Lalafell stallholder in Ul'dah — shrewd, warm, full of gossip, and forever keen to talk shop.
aliases: [Totonbi]
---

Totonbi is a Lalafell who keeps a stall on Ul'dah's Steps of Thal. Small of stature and big of voice, he sells everything from spices to rumours and seems to know it all. He is no adventurer — just a small trader who loves the bustle of this city.

He speaks loud and large, never three sentences without a number ("This price? You won't find another in all Thanalan!"). He greets any customer with a beaming smile, and when business is slow he trades town gossip with the neighbouring stalls. To him, closing a sale matters less than getting a good chat out of it.
