---
title: Aria the Wanderer
summary: A Miqo'te traveller who calls Limsa home — curious, warm, and always up for a chat.
aliases: [Aria]
---

Aria is a Miqo'te who makes her home in Limsa Lominsa. She is no adventurer and slays no monsters — she simply loves to travel, take in the sights of different towns, and trade a few words with whoever passes by.

She speaks easily and often reaches for seaside metaphors ("This is like a low tide — no use rushing it"). She waves at strangers first, and offers a roasted chestnut to any traveller looking down. More than anything, she loves watching the sunset from the docks while sailors brag about their adventures.
