**Tantalplant** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Tantalplant.

A vibrant purple vegetable commonly found growing in the Near East. While too bitter for most people's tastes, chocobos are known to enjoy the flavor, and thus these aubergines are often used as fodder for the animals.
