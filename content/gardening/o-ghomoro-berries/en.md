**O'Ghomoro Berries** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: O'Ghomoro Berries.

A bitter fruit found growing on the volcanic soil of O'Ghomoro. While typically avoided by the kobolds, it is a favorite amongst chocobos, and is known to deepen the blue hues of their feathers.
