**Mamook Pear** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Mamook Pear.

A bitter fruit found growing in the arid soils of Mamook. While typically avoided by the Mamool Ja themselves, it is a favorite amongst chocobos, and is known to deepen the green hues of their feathers.
