**Xelphatol Apple** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Xelphatol Apple.

A bitter fruit found growing in the Ixali homelands. While typically avoided by the Ixal themselves, it is a favorite amongst chocobos, and is known to deepen the red hues of their feathers.
