title: "Curiel Root"

**Curiel Root** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Curiel Root.

A parsnip-like tuber found growing on the windswept islands of the northern seas. While too bitter for most people's tastes, chocobos are known to enjoy the flavor, and thus the root is often used as fodder for the animals.
