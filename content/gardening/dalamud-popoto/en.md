title: "Dalamud Popoto"
summary: "A plant residents can grow."
---

**Dalamud Popoto** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Dalamud Popoto.

Hailing also from the western continent, this cousin to the common popoto is lurid red in color.
