title: "Shroud Tea Leaves"
summary: "A plant residents can grow."
---

**Shroud Tea Leaves** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Shroud Tea Leaves.

Picked from plants that grow wild in the Black Shroud, these leaves are extremely bitter and are not ideal for brewing tea.
