**Mandragora Queen** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Mandragora Queen.

Though wed to the Tomato King in a move to strengthen the bond of houses Tomato and Mandragora, the queen longs to steal away with her one true love—the Eggplant Knight. What will become of this forbidden romance!? Use item to acquire the Mandragora Queen minion and find out!
