**Pahsana Fruit** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Pahsana Fruit.

A bright-red tomato-like fruit brought back from the New World by Lominsan explorers. While too tangy for most people's tastes, chocobos are known to enjoy the flavor, and thus the fruits are often used as fodder for the animals.
