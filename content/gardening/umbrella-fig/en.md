**Umbrella Fig** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Umbrella Fig.

An ornamental plant native to the islands of the south seas, given its name by Lalafellin clans who used its prodigious leaves in lieu of umbrellas.
