title: "Coerthan Tea Leaves"
summary: "A plant residents can grow."
---

**Coerthan Tea Leaves** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Coerthan Tea Leaves.

Considerably smaller than Thanalan tea leaves, more than double the amount of Ishgardian tea leaves are required to brew a single cup of tea.
