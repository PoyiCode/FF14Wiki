**Doman Plum** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Doman Plum.

A hard, bitter fruit found in the forests of Far Eastern Doma. It is a favorite amongst chocobos, and is known to lighten the red hues of their feathers.
