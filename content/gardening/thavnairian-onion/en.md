**Thavnairian Onion** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Thavnairian Onion.

A pungent, tear-inducing vegetable that thrives in the alkali sands of Near Eastern Thavnair. It is a favorite amongst chocobos.
