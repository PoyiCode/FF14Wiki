title: "Mirror Apple"
summary: "A plant residents can grow."
---

**Mirror Apple** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Mirror Apple.

A sweet yet sour variety of apples native to Coerthas. When polished, the skins become so shiny it is said one can see their own reflection in them.
