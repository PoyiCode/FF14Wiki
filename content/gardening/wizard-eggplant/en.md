title: "Wizard Eggplant"
summary: "A plant residents can grow."
---

**Wizard Eggplant** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Wizard Eggplant.

A firm purple-skinned vegetable indigenous to the mountains of the Near East. A common wives' tale claims if you eat too many of this vegetable, you will turn into one.
