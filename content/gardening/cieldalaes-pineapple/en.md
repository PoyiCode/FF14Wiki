**Cieldalaes Pineapple** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Cieldalaes Pineapple.

A bitter fruit found growing on the Cieldalaes—a remote archipelago in the Rhotano Sea. It is a favorite amongst chocobos, and is known to lighten the blue hues of their feathers.
