**Ala Mhigan Mustard** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Ala Mhigan Mustard.

When dried and ground into a fine powder, this yellow-tinged spice can add quite the kick to any dish.
