**Dzemael Tomato** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Dzemael Tomato.

A rare crossbreed of ruby tomato developed and perfected by House Dzemael botanists.
