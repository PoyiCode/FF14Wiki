**Valfruit** is a crop a resident can grow in a garden plot or flowerpot at home, to harvest or simply to enjoy tending. In-game name: Valfruit.

A bitter berry found growing on the distant Isle of Val. It is a favorite amongst chocobos, and is known to lighten the green hues of their feathers.
