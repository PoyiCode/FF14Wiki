**flame hatchling** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: flame hatchling.

Summon your flame hatchling minion. Not more than a fortnight out of the shell, but his blood already runs Immortal Flames black.
