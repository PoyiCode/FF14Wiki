---
title: "Namingway"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Namingway** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Namingway.

Summon your Namingway minion. Do not think this means you can now change your character name.
