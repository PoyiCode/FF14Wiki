**Hoary the Snowman** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Hoary the Snowman.

Summon your Hoary the Snowman minion. Hoary the Snowman was a jolly happy soul, without a pipe, and without a nose, and two eyes made out of something other than coal. Probably slag.
