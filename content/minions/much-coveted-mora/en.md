**much-coveted Mora** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: much-coveted Mora.

Summon forth your much-coveted Mora minion. Few wavekin have ever been the object of so much anticipation, only to be followed by such crushing disappointment.
