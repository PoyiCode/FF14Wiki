**wind-up Sadu** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Sadu.

Summon your wind-up Sadu minion. Will most likely not call any of your companions a stunted chuluu.
