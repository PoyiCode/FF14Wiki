**wind-up Ultros** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Ultros.

Summon your wind-up Ultros minion. A fraction of the size, yet every bit as annoying as the real thing. Be forewarned: once Ultros shows up, he's liable to stick around.
