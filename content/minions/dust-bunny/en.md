**dust bunny** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: dust bunny.

Summon your dust bunny minion. Despite the striking similarities, spriggans are not related to rabbits. But they have been known to eat them.
