**wind-up Nanamo** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Nanamo.

Summon your wind-up Nanamo minion. She vehemently refuses to sit upon any shoulder but Raubahn's...and yours.
