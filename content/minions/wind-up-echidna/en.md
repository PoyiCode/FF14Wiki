**wind-up Echidna** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Echidna.

Summon your wind-up Echidna minion. A remarkably lifelike recreation of the shape-shifting seductress. Perhaps a tad *too* lifelike...
