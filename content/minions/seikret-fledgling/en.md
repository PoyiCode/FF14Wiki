---

**Seikret fledgling** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Seikret fledgling.

Summon your Seikret fledgling minion. Dreams of the day it attains rugged good looks and colorful feathers.
