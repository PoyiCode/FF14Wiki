**wind-up Ixion** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Ixion.

Summon your wind-up Ixion minion. A horse is a horse, of course. Except when it is Ixion.
