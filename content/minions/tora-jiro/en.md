**Tora-jiro** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Tora-jiro.

Summon your Tora-jiro minion. His name suggests that this tiny tiger cub has an older brother. His parents deny everything.
