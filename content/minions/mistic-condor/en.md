---
title: "Mistic condor"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Mistic condor** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Mistic condor.

Summon your Mistic condor minion. Feed it only perfectly cooked flesh, lest you incur its displeasure.
