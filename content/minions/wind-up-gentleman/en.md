---
title: "wind-up gentleman"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**wind-up gentleman** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up gentleman.

Summon your wind-up gentleman minion. If “clockwork Hildibrand” doesn't quite roll off the tongue, both “minion of enquiry” and “mammet extraordinaire” are acceptable alternatives.
