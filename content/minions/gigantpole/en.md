**gigantpole** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: gigantpole.

Summon your gigantpole minion. Yes, it can survive for hours on end without any water, and yes, it can fly. Your argument is invalid.
