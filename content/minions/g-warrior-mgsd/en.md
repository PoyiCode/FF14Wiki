**G-Warrior MGSD** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: G-Warrior MGSD.

Summon your G-Warrior MGSD minion. The “MGSD” part of its name supposedly stands for “Master Garlond Superior Design,” or something along those lines.
