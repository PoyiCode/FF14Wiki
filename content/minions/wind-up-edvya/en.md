**wind-up Edvya** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Edvya.

Summon your wind-up Edvya minion. Sometimes moonlights as a miller girl, much to her father the sultan's chagrin.
