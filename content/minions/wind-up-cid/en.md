**wind-up Cid** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Cid.

Summon your wind-up Cid minion. When asked, if he had a granddaughter, what would he like to see her named, the Ironworks founder replied, “Gertrude.”
