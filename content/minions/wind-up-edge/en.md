**wind-up Edge** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Edge.

Summon forth your wind-up Edge minion. Based on one of the few people who had the chance to be a prince *and* a ninja, which seems like too much fun to squeeze into one lifetime.
