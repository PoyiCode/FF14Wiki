**Dáinsleif F1** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Dáinsleif F1.

Summon your Dáinsleif F1 minion. Does not form the head. Or any other part of the body, for that matter.
