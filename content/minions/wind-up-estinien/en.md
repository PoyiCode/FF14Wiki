**wind-up Estinien** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Estinien.

Summon your wind-up Estinien minion. Absolutely, positively not possessed by an evil dragon bent on ridding the world of mankind.
