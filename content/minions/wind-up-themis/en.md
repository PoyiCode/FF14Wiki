**wind-up Themis** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Themis.

Summon your wind-up Themis minion. A minion as loyal as your star is bright—a minion not bound by the confines of this world, or any other.
