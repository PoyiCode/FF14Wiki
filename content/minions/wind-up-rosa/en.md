**wind-up Rosa** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Rosa.

Summon forth your wind-up Rosa minion. Based on the central corner of a one-sided love triangle, if such a thing is possible.
