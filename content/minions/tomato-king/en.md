**Tomato King** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Tomato King.

Summon your Tomato King minion. Has issued a royal decree for the public defenestration of any and all who mistakenly claim the king is a vegetable.
