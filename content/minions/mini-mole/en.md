**mini mole** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: mini mole.

Summon your mini mole minion. When not dining on a protein-rich diet of earthworms and grubs, he dreams of making mountains.
