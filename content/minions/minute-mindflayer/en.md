**minute mindflayer** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: minute mindflayer.

Summon your minute mindflayer minion. Delights folk with its cuteness even as it fills them with an inexplicable sense of dread.
