---
title: "Trike"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Trike** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Trike.

Summon your Trike minion. Very affectionate, but be prepared to make a quick sidestep if it comes running to greet you.
