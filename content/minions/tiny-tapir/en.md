**tiny tapir** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: tiny tapir.

Summon your tiny tapir minion. Natives of the New World claim that tapirs grow by devouring the dreams of their unsuspecting owners. And now you know what to expect.
