**mummy's little mummy** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: mummy's little mummy.

Summon your mummy's little mummy minion. While as cute as a button, remember, mummies are ultimately embalmed corpses damned to eternal thralldom in the land of the living. Have fun.
