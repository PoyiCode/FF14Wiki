title: "Brina"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Brina** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Brina.

Summon your Brina minion. Collectors warn that one who wishes to complete the set should be well versed in the art of combat.
