---
title: "Pumpkin Butler"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Pumpkin Butler** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Pumpkin Butler.

Summon your Pumpkin Butler minion. Oft-forgotten member of the Mandragoras, if the Pumpkin Butler harbors any ill will for those who have so easily forsaken him, he does a fine job of hiding it.
