**Cerberpup** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Cerberpup.

Summon your Cerberpup minion. With three wet little noses and three sets of adorable staring eyes, what's not to love? Aside from all of the teeth, obviously.
