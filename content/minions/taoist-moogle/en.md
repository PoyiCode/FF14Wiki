**Taoist moogle** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Taoist moogle.

Summon your Taoist moogle minion. The mogpath that can be spoken of is not the true mogpath, kupo.
