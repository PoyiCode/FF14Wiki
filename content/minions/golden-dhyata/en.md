**Golden Dhyata** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Golden Dhyata.

Summon your Golden Dhyata minion. Is it a bird? Is it a fish? No, it's a translation device!
