**wind-up Godbert** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Godbert.

Summon your wind-up Godbert minion. No less gentlemanly for its compact size, this mammet stands ever ready to do what only a Manderville can.
