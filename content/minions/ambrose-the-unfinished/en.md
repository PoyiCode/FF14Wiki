**Ambrose the Unfinished** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Ambrose the Unfinished.

Summon your Ambrose the Unfinished minion. Unfinished it may be, but it is certainly not unwilling.
