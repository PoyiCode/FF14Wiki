**ephemeral necromancer** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: ephemeral necromancer.

Summon your ephemeral necromancer minion. If it could, it would summon forth an army of equally adorable undead.
