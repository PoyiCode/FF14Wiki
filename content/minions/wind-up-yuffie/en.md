**wind-up Yuffie** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Yuffie.

Before you stands a rare bloom: the single white rose of Wutai—the one and only Yuffie! Summon your wind-up Yuffie minion.
