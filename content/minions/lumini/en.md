title: "lumini"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**lumini** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: lumini.

Summon your lumini minion. This floating ball of fleshy horror was modeled after an Ea who failed to regain full corporeality.
