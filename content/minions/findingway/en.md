---
title: "Findingway"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Findingway** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Findingway.

Summon your Findingway minion. Can find his own way without you, but chooses to tag along.
