**wind-up Leviathan** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Leviathan.

Summon your wind-up Leviathan minion. Your flesh cannot feed this minion of the deep.
