title: "wind-up Kain"

---

**wind-up Kain** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Kain.

Summon your wind-up Kain minion. A few moments may be needed to allow him to regain control of himself.
