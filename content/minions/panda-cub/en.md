**panda cub** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: panda cub.

Summon your panda cub minion. Untrained in arts both martial and magical, but as scrumptiously adorable as chicken glazed in a tangy orange reduction.
