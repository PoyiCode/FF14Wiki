**wind-up Tarutaru** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Tarutaru.

Summon your wind-up Tarutaru minion. Remarks relating to a certain people's stature and the diminutiveness thereof are best kept to oneself.
