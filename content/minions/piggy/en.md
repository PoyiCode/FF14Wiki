---
title: "Piggy"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Piggy** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Piggy.

Summon your Piggy minion. Many have tried casting Esuna on what they thought was a struggling adventurer, only to find they had wasted their magic.
