**Nana bear** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Nana bear.

Summon your Nana bear minion. Pundits claim bears to be the deadliest beasts in the realm—obviously having never stared into *this* cute cub's nebulous eyes.
