title: "wind-up sasquatch"

---

**wind-up sasquatch** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up sasquatch.

Summon your wind-up sasquatch minion. It thankfully cannot pelt you with its steaming stool.
