title: "wind-up sylph"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**wind-up sylph** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up sylph.

Summon your wind-up sylph minion. If he could speak, it would be in the third person.
