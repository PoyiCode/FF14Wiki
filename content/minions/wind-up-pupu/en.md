**wind-up PuPu** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up PuPu.

Summon your wind-up PuPu minion. Will bleed you dry of elixirs if you aren't careful.
