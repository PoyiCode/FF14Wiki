**Fenrir pup** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Fenrir pup.

Summon your Fenrir pup minion. This stray hoarhound pup is as feisty as the leader of his pack, and just as aloof.
