title: "Malone"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Malone** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Malone.

Summon your Malone minion. If it weren't following you around, it would be tearing hapless adventurers apart on the Diadem.
