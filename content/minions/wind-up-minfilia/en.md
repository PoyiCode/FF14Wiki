**wind-up Minfilia** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Minfilia.

Summon your wind-up Minfilia minion. Unlike the real Minfilia, this one actually accompanies you on your adventures.
