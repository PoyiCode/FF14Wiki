**wind-up leader** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up leader.

Summon your wind-up leader minion, who will follow you unquestioningly—for coin and country; for serenity, purity, sanctity—till sea swallows all.
