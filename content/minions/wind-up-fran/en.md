**wind-up Fran** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Fran.

Summon your wind-up Fran minion. Stilettos guaranteed not to impede progress through markedly rough terrain such as jungles, bogs, and abandoned monasteries serving as prisons for extra-dimensional forces of evil.
