**wind-up Shantotto** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Shantotto.

Summon your wind-up Shantotto minion. But remember: If you fail to treat her right, she will set your pants alight.
