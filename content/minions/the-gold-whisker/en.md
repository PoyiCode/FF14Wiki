---

**the Gold Whisker** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: the Gold Whisker.

Summon your Gold Whisker minion. Beckons you to enter his barrel of sin, but don't go in.
