---
title: "Prince Lunatender"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Prince Lunatender** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Prince Lunatender.

Summon your Prince Lunatender minion. Use as a decoration at risk of suffering puncture wounds—lots of them.
