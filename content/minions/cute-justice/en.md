title: "Cute Justice"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Cute Justice** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Cute Justice.

Summon your Cute Justice minion. Rocket Punch parts may present a choking hazard. Not for children under the age of two.
