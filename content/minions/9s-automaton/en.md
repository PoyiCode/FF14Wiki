**9S automaton** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: 9S automaton.

Summon your 9S automaton minion. For when you need the company of a compact version of an advanced combat android dressed in a distinctive subculture style.
