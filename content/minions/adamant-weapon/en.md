**Adamant Weapon** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Adamant Weapon.

Summon your Adamant Weapon minion. A diamond by any other name would shine as bright—at least when scale is accounted for.
