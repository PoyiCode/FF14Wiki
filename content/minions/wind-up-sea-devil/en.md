**wind-up Sea Devil** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Sea Devil.

Summon your wind-up Sea Devil minion. A clockwork automaton expertly crafted to resemble the infamous Scarlet Sea Devil, leader of the Coral Tridents.
