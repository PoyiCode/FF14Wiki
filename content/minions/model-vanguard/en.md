**model vanguard** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: model vanguard.

Summon your model vanguard minion. An exact 1:9 scale replica of a ceruleum-powered magitek vanguard (imperial pilot action figure not included).
