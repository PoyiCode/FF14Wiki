**wide-eyed fawn** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wide-eyed fawn.

Summon your wide-eyed fawn minion. His mother was either lost in a terrible hunting accident...or perhaps is just lost.
