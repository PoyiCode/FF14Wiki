**wind-up Porom** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Porom.

Summon forth your wind-up Porom minion. The rosy cheeks and pronounced eyelashes indicate that this is the female half of a matching pair.
