**wind-up Golbez** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Golbez.

Summon your wind-up Golbez minion. Too small to conceive of and carry out a mass invasion of the Source...or is he?
