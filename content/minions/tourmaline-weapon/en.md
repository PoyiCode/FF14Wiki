**Tourmaline Weapon** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Tourmaline Weapon.

Summon your Tourmaline Weapon minion. Stands ready to lend you a helping hand or six.
