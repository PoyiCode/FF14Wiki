---
title: "quetzal"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**quetzal** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: quetzal.

Summon your quetzal minion. Do not be fooled by its winsome appearance—you are in the presence of *divinity*.
