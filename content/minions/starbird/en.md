title: "starbird"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**starbird** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: starbird.

Summon your starbird minion. Gazing up at the stars in the heavens, it ever sings a song of hope.
