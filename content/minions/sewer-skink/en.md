**sewer skink** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: sewer skink.

Summon your sewer skink minion. When it comes to keeping aqueducts free of creepy-crawlies, this lightning-tongued lizard is the hero of the day.
