**heavy hatchling** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: heavy hatchling.

Summon your heavy hatchling minion. Currently, “baby fat” is the prevailing excuse for this cherubic chick's gigant-like girth.
