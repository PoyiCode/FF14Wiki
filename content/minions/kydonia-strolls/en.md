**Kydonia Strolls** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Kydonia Strolls.

Summon your Kydonia Strolls minion. For when you need a stony face to stare at you unflinchingly.
