**lesser panda** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: lesser panda.

Summon your lesser panda minion. Lesser only in size to normal pandas. And in life expectancy. And intelligence. And fluffiness. Well, perhaps not fluffiness.
