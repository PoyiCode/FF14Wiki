title: "tiny rat"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**tiny rat** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: tiny rat.

Summon your tiny rat minion. He may infest your granaries, and he may carry the plague, but he's *simply too adorable* to condemn to the life-ending jaws of a metal trap.
