**gaelikitten** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: gaelikitten.

Summon your gaelikitten minion. Rumors that the gods kill one of these adorable creatures every time a young man thinks pleasurable thoughts are unfounded.
