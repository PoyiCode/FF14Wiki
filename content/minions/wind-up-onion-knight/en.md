**wind-up onion knight** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up onion knight.

Summon your magic wind-up onion knight minion. An accurate representation of the soldiers who once made up the greater portion of the Allagan Imperial Army.
