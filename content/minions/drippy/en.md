---
title: "Drippy"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Drippy** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Drippy.

Summon your Drippy minion. With a will to wander the world, one wonders whether said world will welcome this well-meaning watery whelp.
