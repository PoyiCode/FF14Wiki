title: "wind-up moon"

---

**wind-up moon** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up moon.

Summon your wind-up moon minion. /poke to adjust elevation. /point to inform others of its location. /blowkiss to get strange looks from passersby.
