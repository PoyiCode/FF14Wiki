**wind-up hobgoblin** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up hobgoblin.

Summon your wind-up hobgoblin minion. Crafted in the likeness of your mother...or so certain dwarves would say.
