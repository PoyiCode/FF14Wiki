**naughty nanka** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: naughty nanka.

Summon your naughty nanka minion. Like its parent, the ninki nanka, this slippery wavekin will breed like a rabbit if left unsupervised.
