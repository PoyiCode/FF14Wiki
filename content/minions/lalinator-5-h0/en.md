**Lalinator 5.H0** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Lalinator 5.H0.

Summon your Lalinator 5.H0 minion. It doesn't feel pity, or remorse, or fear. And it absolutely will not stop, ever, until you have been lali-ho'd.
