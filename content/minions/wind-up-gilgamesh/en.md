**wind-up Gilgamesh** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Gilgamesh.

Summon your wind-up Gilgamesh minion. Several arms appear to be missing, but that does not seem to otherwise hinder basic functionality.
