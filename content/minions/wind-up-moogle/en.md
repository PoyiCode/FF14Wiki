**wind-up moogle** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up moogle.

Summon your wind-up moogle minion. With its button nose and fluffy pom, this clockwork moogle will make you the envy of all your friends.
