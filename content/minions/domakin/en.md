title: "domakin"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**domakin** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: domakin.

Summon your domakin minion. Close, but not a warrior with the body of a mortal and soul of a dragon.
