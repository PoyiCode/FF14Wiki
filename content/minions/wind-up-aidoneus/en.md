**wind-up Aidoneus** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Aidoneus.

Summon your wind-up Aidoneus minion. If sighs could kill, you'd be dead a thousand times over.
