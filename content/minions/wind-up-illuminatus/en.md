**wind-up Illuminatus** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: wind-up Illuminatus.

Summon your wind-up Illuminatus minion. Comes complete with two hands, both blue as an Illuminati baby's bum.
