title: "Syldrion-class insubmersible"
summary: "A minion that follows you around."
---

**Syldrion-class insubmersible** is a minion — a small companion that trots along beside a resident, purely for company and charm, and that players love to collect and show off. In-game name: Syldrion-class insubmersible.

Summon your Syldrion-class insubmersible minion. Unlike the craft on which it is modeled, it will break immediately upon coming into contact with water.
