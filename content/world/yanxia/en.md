**Yanxia** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Othard** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Yanxia.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Clouds（15%）, Rain（10%）, Fog（10%）, Showers（5%）
