---

**The Sea of Clouds** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Abalathia's Spine** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Sea of Clouds.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Fair Skies（30%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（10%）, Wind（10%）, Umbral Wind（10%）
