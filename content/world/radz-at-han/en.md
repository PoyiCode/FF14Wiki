**Radz-at-Han** is an aetheryte in **Ilsabard**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: Radz-at-Han.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clouds（20%）, Rain（15%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Fog（10%）
