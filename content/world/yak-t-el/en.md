**Yak T'el** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Yok Tural** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Yak T'el.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Clouds（15%）, Fog（15%）, Rain（15%）
