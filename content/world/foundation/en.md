title: "Foundation"
summary: "An aetheryte (teleport point) in Coerthas."
---

**Foundation** is an aetheryte in **Coerthas**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: Foundation.

Common weather here: Snow（60%）, Clouds（15%）, Fair Skies（10%）, Fog（10%）, Clear Skies（5%）
