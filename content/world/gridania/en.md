---
title: Gridania
summary: A forest city hidden in the Black Shroud, living in harmony with nature and its elementals.
aliases: [Gridania, The Forest City]
---

Gridania sits deep within the **Black Shroud**, a city of waterways, wooden bridges and treehouses split into a new town and an old one. Its people live alongside the forest's elementals, and prize harmony with nature in all things — even felling timber or drawing water follows a rite.

Residents are mostly gentle, courteous and reverent of nature. The air smells of damp earth and leaves; at dawn one hears birdsong and the distant snap of bowstrings. For a resident, it is a fine place to stroll, reflect, and listen to the whisper of the forest.

Vistas here: Apkallu Falls, The Carline Canopy, The Lancers' Guild, The Leatherworkers' Guild
