**Heritage Found** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Xak Tural** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Heritage Found.

Common weather here: Umbral Static（50%）, Clouds（20%）, Fog（15%）, Fair Skies（5%）, Rain（5%）, Thunderstorms（5%）
