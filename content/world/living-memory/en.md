**Living Memory** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Unlost World** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Living Memory.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（60%）, Clouds（20%）, Rain（10%）, Fog（10%）
