**Mare Lamentorum** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **The Sea of Stars** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Mare Lamentorum.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（70%）, Umbral Wind（15%）, Moon Dust（15%）
