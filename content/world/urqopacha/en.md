**Urqopacha** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Yok Tural** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Urqopacha.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（30%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Clouds（20%）, Fog（10%）, Wind（10%）, Snow（10%）
