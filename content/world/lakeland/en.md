title: "Lakeland"
summary: "An explorable zone in Norvrandt."
---

**Lakeland** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Norvrandt** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Lakeland.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Clouds（15%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）, Thunderstorms（5%）
