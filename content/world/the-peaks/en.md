**The Peaks** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Gyr Abania** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Peaks.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（50%）, Clouds（15%）, Clear Skies（10%）, Fog（10%）, Wind（10%）, Dust Storms（5%）
