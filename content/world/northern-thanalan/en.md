---

**Northern Thanalan** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Thanalan** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Northern Thanalan.

Common weather here: Fog（50%）, Clouds（30%）, Fair Skies（15%）, Clear Skies（5%）
