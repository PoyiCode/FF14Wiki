**The Azim Steppe** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Othard** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Azim Steppe.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（25%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（8%）, Rain（7%）, Gales（5%）, Wind（5%）
