**Kholusia** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Norvrandt** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Kholusia.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clouds（15%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Gales（10%）, Rain（10%）, Fog（10%）
