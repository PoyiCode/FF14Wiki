**Elpis** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **The World Unsundered** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Elpis.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（45%）, Clouds（25%）, Umbral Wind（15%）, Clear Skies（15%）
