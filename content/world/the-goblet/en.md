title: "The Goblet"
summary: "A residential district in Thanalan."
---

**The Goblet** is one of Eorzea's residential districts in **Thanalan**, where residents own houses and apartments and make their homes. A peaceful neighbourhood to live, garden and host friends. In-game name: The Goblet.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（40%）, Clouds（25%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（5%）
