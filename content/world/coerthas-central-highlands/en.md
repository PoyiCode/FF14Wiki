**Coerthas Central Highlands** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Coerthas** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Coerthas Central Highlands.

Common weather here: Snow（40%）, Blizzards（20%）, Clouds（15%）, Fair Skies（10%）, Fog（10%）, Clear Skies（5%）
