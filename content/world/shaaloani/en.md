**Shaaloani** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Xak Tural** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Shaaloani.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（45%）, Clouds（20%）, Dust Storms（15%）, Gales（15%）, Clear Skies（5%）
