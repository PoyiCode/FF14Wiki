**Labyrinthos** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **The Northern Empty** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Labyrinthos.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（45%）, Clouds（25%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Rain（15%）
