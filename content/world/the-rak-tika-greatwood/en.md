**The Rak'tika Greatwood** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Norvrandt** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Rak'tika Greatwood.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Clouds（15%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）, Umbral Wind（10%）
