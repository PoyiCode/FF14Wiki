**Kozama'uka** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Yok Tural** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Kozama'uka.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（35%）, Clear Skies（25%）, Clouds（15%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）, Showers（5%）
