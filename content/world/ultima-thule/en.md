title: "Ultima Thule"
summary: "An explorable zone in The Sea of Stars."
---

**Ultima Thule** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **The Sea of Stars** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Ultima Thule.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（70%）, Astromagnetic Storms（15%）, Umbral Wind（15%）
