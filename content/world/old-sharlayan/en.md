**Old Sharlayan** is an aetheryte in **The Northern Empty**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: Old Sharlayan.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clouds（20%）, Fog（15%）, Snow（15%）, Clear Skies（10%）
