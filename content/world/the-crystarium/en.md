**The Crystarium** is an aetheryte in **Norvrandt**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: The Crystarium.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Clouds（15%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）, Thunderstorms（5%）
