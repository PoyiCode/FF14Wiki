**Western Thanalan** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Thanalan** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Western Thanalan.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（40%）, Clouds（25%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（5%）
