**Western La Noscea** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **La Noscea** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Western La Noscea.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Clouds（20%）, Fog（10%）, Wind（10%）, Gales（10%）
