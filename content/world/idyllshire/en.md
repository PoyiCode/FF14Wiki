summary: "An aetheryte (teleport point) in Dravania."
---

**Idyllshire** is an aetheryte in **Dravania**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: Idyllshire.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Fair Skies（30%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）, Showers（10%）
