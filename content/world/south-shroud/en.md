**South Shroud** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **The Black Shroud** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: South Shroud.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（30%）, Clear Skies（30%）, Thunder（15%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（5%）, Thunderstorms（5%）, Fog（5%）
