**Amh Araeng** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Norvrandt** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Amh Araeng.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（45%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Clouds（15%）, Dust Storms（10%）, Heat Waves（10%）
