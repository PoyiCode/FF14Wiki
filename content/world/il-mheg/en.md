**Il Mheg** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Norvrandt** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Il Mheg.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clouds（15%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Rain（10%）, Fog（10%）, Thunderstorms（10%）
