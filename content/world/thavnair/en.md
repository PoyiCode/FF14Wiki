**Thavnair** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Ilsabard** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Thavnair.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clouds（20%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）, Showers（5%）
