**The Ruby Sea** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Othard** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Ruby Sea.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（25%）, Clouds（15%）, Thunder（10%）, Wind（10%）
