**Southern Thanalan** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Thanalan** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Southern Thanalan.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（40%）, Heat Waves（20%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（10%）
