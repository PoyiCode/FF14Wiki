summary: "An explorable zone in Gyr Abania."
---

**The Fringes** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Gyr Abania** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Fringes.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（45%）, Clouds（20%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Fog（10%）, Thunder（10%）
