**Tuliyollal** is an aetheryte in **Yok Tural**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: Tuliyollal.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（40%）, Fair Skies（40%）, Fog（10%）, Clouds（5%）, Rain（5%）
