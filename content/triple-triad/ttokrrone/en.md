**Ttokrrone** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ttokrrone.

A tural vidraal whispered of in tones of dread, the “Serpentlord” has crushed entire villages beneath its scaled bulk, and even humbled a seasoned viper intent on taking its head. Named in the Hhetsarro tongue, it is the dweller beneath the shifting sands and the swallower of rroneek.
