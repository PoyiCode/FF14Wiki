**Diamond Weapon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Diamond Weapon.

The final entry in the VIIth Legion's Weapon series incorporated data collected by units that fought the Warrior of Light, and it was equipped with the newly developed Overmind system. Had the Garlean Empire succeeded in unleashing the Diamond Weapon's destructive capabilities upon Eorzea, the results would have been catastrophic.
