**Rofocale** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rofocale.

Chariot, speech, and attire all suggest this twisted denizen of Rabanastre was once a high-ranking general in the city's standing army, transformed by the power of the Duma.
