**Zodiark** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Zodiark.

The eldest and most powerful of primals, created by the ancients to forestall the Final Days. Zodiark succeeded in His task of restoring balance to the star, and prevented the devastation which so many feared. Sundered by Hydaelyn's hand, He was never to reclaim His full and unbroken might.
