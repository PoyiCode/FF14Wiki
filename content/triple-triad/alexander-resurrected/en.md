**Alexander Resurrected** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Alexander Resurrected.

This echo was formed from the memories of the celestial avatar, Alexander. An embodiment of Vana'diel's will, legends tell of the apocalyptic war it waged against its dark counterpart, Odin.
