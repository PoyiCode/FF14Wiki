**Shadow Lord** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Shadow Lord.

This echo was formed from the memories of Vana'diel's tyrannical Shadow Lord. The original was remembered as a terrifying presence who rallied the beastmen against the nations of man in a war of wrath and vengeance.
