**Fat Chocobo** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Fat Chocobo.

A chocobo whose insatiable appetite and utter lack of willpower have resulted in a body more fit for a behemoth than a bird. Goblin legend has it this oversized avian is worshipped as a god in the world below, but no compelling evidence has been found to support this myth.
