**Maulskull** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Maulskull.

Thought to have been created by the Yok Huy for quarrying purposes, Maulskull became a sentry when its operational runes were partially overwritten by persons unknown. With its deadly, hammer-like head, it inflicts punishing blows upon any foolish enough to trespass into the Skydeep Cenote.
