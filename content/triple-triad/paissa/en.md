**Paissa** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Paissa.

Discovered on the floating islands of the Sea of Clouds, paissas are mischievous, fur-covered beastkin with barrel-like bodies and saucer-sized eyes. There is a theory gaining popularity among naturalists that the paissa is closely related to the opo-opo; the Vanu, however, are content to simply classify these creatures as incarnations of pure idiocy.
