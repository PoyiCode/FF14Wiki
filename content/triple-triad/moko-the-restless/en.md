**Moko the Restless** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Moko the Restless.

A respected general during the Age of Blood, Moko was celebrated for cutting down countless enemy commanders with unparalleled skill. Fearful of the people's loyalty to the fierce warrior, Moko's liege saw fit to have him beheaded by his own blade as he slept. His spirit now inhabits the suit of armor he once wore into battle, awaiting the chance to slake its thirst for revenge.
