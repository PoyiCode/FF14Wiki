**Geryon the Steer** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Geryon the Steer.

Descended from a race used as forced labor in bygone Thanalan, Geryon roamed the Eorzean subterrane, driven by the ambition to found his own empire. Alas, he had the misfortune of running into a formidable adventurer in the Sil'dihn waterways, by whose hand his quest came to a premature end.
