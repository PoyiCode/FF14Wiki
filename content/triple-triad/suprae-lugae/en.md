**Suprae-Lugae** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Suprae-Lugae.

Despite engineering himself into a twisted amalgamation of flesh and metal, this magitek inventor was bested by an adventurer in the Tower of Babil. Further misfortune left him as a head without a body—thus did he contrive to attach his gleaming skull to the hulking remains of Barnabas, his “beautiful little boy,” and maximize his offensive capabilities.
