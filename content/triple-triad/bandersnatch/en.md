**Bandersnatch** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Bandersnatch.

A forest-dwelling beastkin, the bandersnatch is a vicious hunter that employs long, curved fangs to rend the flesh of its prey. Though it feeds predominantly on chocobos and other forest creatures, this frenzied carnivore has been known to launch itself at dragons when lost in the throes of uncontrollable bloodlust.
