**Shadowcaster Zeless Gah** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Shadowcaster Zeless Gah.

An Amalj'aa warrior who died some two hundred thirty years ago. Skilled at warding magicks wielded by him alone, he voluntarily joined Ul'dah's efforts to purge the undead. Unfortunately, he was wounded in the course of protecting an ally, and subsequently turned into the very thing that he had sought to vanquish.
