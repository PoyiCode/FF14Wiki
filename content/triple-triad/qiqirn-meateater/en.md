**Qiqirn Meateater** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Qiqirn Meateater.

The Qiqirn tribes of Gyr Abania are an unusually bloodthirsty breed who consume the flesh of animal and man alike. Classified as wild beasts by the Garlean Empire and thus unworthy of contact, these carnivorous savages have been largely ignored even as they continue to terrorize local settlements.
