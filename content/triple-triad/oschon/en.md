**Oschon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Oschon.

Oschon is ruler of the mountains and god of wanderers and vagrants. Depicted as a carefree ranger wielding a bow of yew, the majestic peaks and howling winds of the world are said to be of his divine creation. Oschon is the explorer Deryk's true form, and it was he who brought mortals to the Omphalos to fulfill the Twelve's behest.
