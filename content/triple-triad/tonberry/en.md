**Tonberry** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Tonberry.

Little is known about this inscrutable race of manlike creatures that inhabits the ruins of Nym—a culture that reached its peak during the Fifth Astral Era. Tonberries wear clothing and are adept in the use of complex tools, both of which would seem to suggest a civilized level of intelligence. Recently discovered texts from Nymian times suggest that the green denizens of the Wanderer's Palace are the carriers of a terrible pestilence.
