**Overseer Kanilokka** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Overseer Kanilokka.

A devotee of Zoraal Ja's expansionist ideals, Kanilokka ran a secret laboratory where he labored with others educated in electrope technology to develop new battlefield advancements. Though he perished ere his research could bear fruit, such was his obsession that his spirit refused to pass on.
