**Outrunner** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Outrunner.

These general-use machines were developed to supplement the dwindling labor force after Alexandria's population was left decimated by warfare and calamities. Not only are they adept at everyday chores such as cleaning and maintenance, but they can also offer stiff competition in various card games.
