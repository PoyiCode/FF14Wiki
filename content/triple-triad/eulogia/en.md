**Eulogia** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Eulogia.

An amalgam of the Twelve, Eulogia embodies the love and magnanimity the gods harbor towards mortals. This divine entity presents the Twelve's final challenge, delighting in the evidence of man's potential to thrive in a world divested of deities.
