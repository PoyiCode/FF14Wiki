**Immortal Remains** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Immortal Remains.

Calyx created this amalgamation of memories from the recollections stored in the Meso Terminal. Empowered by electrope devices installed in its chamber, this massive entity was able to manifest shared experiences as physical phenomena, and employ them in devastating attacks.
