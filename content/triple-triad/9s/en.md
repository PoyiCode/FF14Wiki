**9S** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: 9S.

A child encountered in the Copied Factory. Appearing as a young boy clad in black garb, it is in fact an android—an advanced machine designed in imitation of mankind. 9S was witnessed to be controlling machine lifeforms within the factory, though to what end it remains unknown.
