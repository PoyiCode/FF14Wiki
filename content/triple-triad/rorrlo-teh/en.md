**Rorrlo Teh** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rorrlo Teh.

Counted among Ryoqor Terteh's lesser kin, Rorrlo Tehs are sometimes sighted cavorting around the icy heights of Worqor Zormor. In the Yok Huy tongue, their name means “little spirit illuminated by moonlight,” but despite the peaceful image this evokes, many an unwary traveler has found themselves frozen—in jest, of course—by the playful pranksters.
