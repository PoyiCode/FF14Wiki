**Kam'lanaut** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Kam'lanaut.

This echo was formed from the memories of Kam'lanaut, Archduke of Jeuno in Vana'diel. Though hailed as a hero by the people, he is, in truth, one of the last survivors of an ancient race known as the Zilart.
