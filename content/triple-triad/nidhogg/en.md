**Nidhogg** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Nidhogg.

One of the seven great wyrms sired by Midgardsormr, Father of Dragons. Leader of the Dravanian Horde, Nidhogg and his minions have tormented the Holy See for a thousand years in the bloody conflict that has come to be known as the Dragonsong War. The sound of his cry piercing the heavens can strike fear into the heart of the bravest of knights.
