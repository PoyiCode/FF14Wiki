**Hippo Cart** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Hippo Cart.

Sturdy and reliable, the Hippo Riders' speedy carts will deliver anything to anywhere in two shakes of a hippo's tail. Gaze into their hippos' dark, watery eyes, and know that you're in good hands—and hooves! Offer includes a complimentary gulal shower, while supplies last. (This Triad card sponsored by: the Hippo Riders.)
