**Dvergr** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Dvergr.

Occupying the fourth rung of the hierarchy of twelve, this voidsent serves as an artisan for its fiendish kin. Dvergr has a profound understanding of metalwork and goldsmithing, and no few of the armaments wielded by the void's denizens were created in its hellish forge.
