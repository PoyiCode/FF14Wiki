**Azeyma** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Azeyma.

Keeper of the sun and goddess of inquiry, Azeyma is most often depicted as a beautiful noble lady holding a golden fan—and so does she appear in Aglaia. Though this manifestation welcomes curiosity as a patron of inquiry ought, she is also wont to test pilgrims with unbridled ferocity...
