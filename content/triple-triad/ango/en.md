**Ango** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ango.

This Far Eastern relative to the orobon owes its name to its slack-jawed and slow-witted appearance. Originally called “angu uo” in the local tongue, the word for this “imbecilic fish” was eventually shortened to the present “ango.”
