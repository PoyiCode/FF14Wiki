**Ruby Weapon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ruby Weapon.

Created as a successor to the Ultima Weapon, it has inherited the ability to absorb the aether of primals, which is believed to be the source of its extraordinary resilience and deadly weaponry. This magitek monstrosity is further bolstered by a synthetic auracite system, granting it combat capabilities far beyond those of the Allagan warmachina on which it is based.
