**Zelenia** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Zelenia.

Drawing on Sphene's memory data, this mechanical construct was designed to emulate the combat skills of the valiant Zelenia—second-in-command of the royal knights of Alexandria. Zelenia contributed greatly to the defense of the kingdom during the Storm Surge conflict, and was said to display an unrivaled resolve when it came to protecting those under her charge.
