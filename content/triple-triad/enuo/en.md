**Enuo** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Enuo.

This king among voidsent once dwelled in the darkest depths of the Thirteenth's ocean, emerging to seek out the voidgate on the red moon. Were it not for Etheirys's champion and their allies, naught would have stopped Enuo from unleashing their terrible destructive power upon the Source.
