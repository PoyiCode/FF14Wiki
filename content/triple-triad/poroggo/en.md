**Poroggo** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Poroggo.

Poroggos were once lowly toads, transformed into creatures of speech and reason through the magicks of Sharlayan mages. Their intelligence is such that they can even be taught to cast spells. Should poroggos be isolated from other sentient beings, however, their minds gradually regress into a feral state.
