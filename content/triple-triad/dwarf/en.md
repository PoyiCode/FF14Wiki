**Dwarf** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Dwarf.

Well known for their skill at mining, metallurgy, and building all manner of mechanical marvels, the dwarves make their home in the mountains of Kholusia. They are sticklers for tradition, the most peculiar of which is perhaps their use of helmets, for only family members are permitted to see one another without them.
