**Tokkapchi** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Tokkapchi.

Encountered within Saint Mocianne's Arboretum, this shambling horror was discovered to be a korpokkur covered in mud of strangely invigorating properties. The withered leaf upon its head resembles a crown of sorts, which has led some to refer to its domain as “Kingsloam.”
