**Vacuum Suit** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Vacuum Suit.

Developed to withstand the harsh environs of foreign stars, this mech is powerful enough to destroy crystal and perform other labor-intensive tasks for the Cosmic Exploration Initiative. A number of these units have been simplified and prepared as rewards for contributors.
