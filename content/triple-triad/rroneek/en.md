**Rroneek** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rroneek.

This shambling herbivore inhabits the grassy plains of Xak Tural. Though it usually plods along at a slow pace, an enraged rroneek is an unstoppable force, using its massive frame to charge through any obstacle in its path. The vivid markings upon their faces are thought to allow the beasts to identify individuals among the herd.
