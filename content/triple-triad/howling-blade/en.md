**Howling Blade** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Howling Blade.

The Howling Blade is the Arcadion's cruiserweight champion. Long has he defended his title against all comers, combining his spectacular swordplay with a mastery over earth and wind conferred by the soul of Fenrir. Being a “babyface” fighter and a handsome man to boot, he enjoys an overwhelming degree of support from the cheering crowds.
