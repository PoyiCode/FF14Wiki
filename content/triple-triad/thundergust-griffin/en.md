**Thundergust Griffin** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Thundergust Griffin.

An apex predator in the Ninth, this winged beast terrorizes the lands near Treno, swooping down from great heights to seize large prey. Folk who venture outside the barrier must remain on watch for the creature, which soars the levin-wracked skies unperturbed due to its ability to manipulate lightning.
