**Unknown** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Unknown.

It is said that man fears what he does not know, which makes the Unknown all the more horrifying. A ruin of flesh and bone, mangled teeth, and sunken eyes, none can say for certain whence this odious creature came. But perhaps we are better for not knowing the unknown of the Unknown...
