**Nymeia** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Nymeia.

Nymeia, the watcher of celestial bodies and goddess of fate, stood at her brother Althyk's side in Euphrosyne to test the might of mortals. Often depicted as a weaver donning a white, silken veil, her name is appended to the Nymeia lily for the resemblance the flower's petals have to her pale adornment.
