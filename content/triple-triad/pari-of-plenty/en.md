**Pari of Plenty** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Pari of Plenty.

Ancient Corvosi legend tells of a king who, upon defeating this faerie of fire, demanded the production of a flying carpet. This depiction was later popularized in fables like *The Merchant's Tale*, from which she is now most famously known.
