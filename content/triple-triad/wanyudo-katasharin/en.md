**Wanyudo & Katasharin** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Wanyudo & Katasharin.

A mammet-type construction prevalent in the Far East. The cartwheels, adorned with the unsettling faces of a man and woman, are said to resemble mythical apparitions known for stealing the souls of any who look upon them—a design deliberately intended to frighten away cowardly interlopers.
