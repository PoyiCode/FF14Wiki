**Octomammoth** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Octomammoth.

This colossal sea monster made its way into the Isle of Haam via an underwater cave system, where it claimed the Aetherfont as its territory. After being defeated by an adventurer, it became clear that many of the unexplained incidents in local waters could be attributed to the octopus-like fiend.
