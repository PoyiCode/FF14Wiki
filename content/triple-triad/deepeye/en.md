**Deepeye** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Deepeye.

Possessed of one grotesquely huge eye, the otherwise diminutive deepeye is thought to manifest upon the physical plane by investing its essence exclusively in the ocular organs of beings that perished with their eyes wide open. Though scholars agree that it belongs on the lower rungs of the hierarchy of twelve, this voidsent's acute vision and arcane prowess constitute a respectable threat.
