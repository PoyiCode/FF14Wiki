**Raya-O-Senna & A-Ruhn-Senna** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Raya-O-Senna & A-Ruhn-Senna.

“My name may be unfamiliar to you, but doubtless you know of my sister. That would be Kan–E–Senna, Elder Seedseer of Gridania and supreme commander of the Order of the Twin Adder. Together, we are known as the three Seedseers—my sister, myself, and our younger brother, A–Ruhn–Senna.”
