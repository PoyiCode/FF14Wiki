**Flower Basket** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Flower Basket.

It is said these botanical beasts were originally golems created long ago in the Kingdom of Voeburt, conceived by mages to tend the royal gardens. Their present, floral appearance is the work of fae folk who settled there after the kingdom's fall, and indiscriminately bathed the land in pixie magicks. The result: beautiful flower beds as far as the eye can see, as well as a new breed of gardening guardians.
