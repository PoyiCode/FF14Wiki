**Ambrose** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ambrose.

Origenics researchers sought to create loyal undead minions by infusing corpses with an excess of souls. Test subject Ambrose was disobedient to the last, likely due to the fact that in life he was a member of the resistance organization Oblivion.
