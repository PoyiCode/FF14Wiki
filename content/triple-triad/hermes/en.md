**Hermes** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Hermes.

Hermes, chief overseer of Elpis, in a transformed state. As a skilled crafter of avian creations, he is naturally well versed in wind-aspected magicks, and the herald's staff he carries allows him to commune with Meteion and her sisters as they travel the universe.
