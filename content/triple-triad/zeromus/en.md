**Zeromus** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Zeromus.

Zeromus is a voidsent of incalculable power, born on the moon of the Thirteenth from Zodiark's residual aether. Infused with the essence of the great wyrm Azdaja and consumed by a desire to return “home,” the entity bides its time in Darkness, building strength to rupture the barrier betwixt worlds.
