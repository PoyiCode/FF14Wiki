**Sephirot** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Sephirot.

Sephirot was revered as the world's first sacred tree, elevated to godhood by the belief of its devoted worshipers. This towering pillar of the Warring Triad is capable of inducing spontaneous growth, allowing the eikon to enlarge its own form to enormous proportions.
