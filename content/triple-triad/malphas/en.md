**Malphas** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Malphas.

Occupying the fifth rung of the hierarchy of twelve, this voidsent spins twisted filaments of aether to manipulate matter. In the Clyteum, they stitched scrap and steel into a phalanx of vicious guards in a bid to fell the Warrior of Light and their comrades.
