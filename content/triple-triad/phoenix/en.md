**Phoenix** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Phoenix.

This fiery entity is said to have manifested in the sky following the Battle of Carteneau. Although memories of that time were distorted by the effects of the Calamity, a number of survivors swear to have witnessed the immortal Phoenix, legendary symbol of rebirth, rise up and rescue Eorzea from certain annihilation.
