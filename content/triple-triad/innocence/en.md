**Innocence** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Innocence.

Corrupted by the power of a Lightwarden whilst he was yet a babe in the womb, Lord Vauthry, ruler of Eulmore, underwent a drastic metamorphosis when that power was awoken. He assumed the form of “Innocence,” an entity whose radiant might and presence would eclipse all Wardens that had come before.
