**Ravana** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ravana.

Wrath of the Colony and the god and savior of the Gnath tribe. A proud warrior and conqueror, Lord Ravana exalts strength and abhors weakness in any form. Believing the rite of combat to be sacred, he revels in battle with worthy opponents while wielding the Chandrahas—legendary blades of moonlight that obey his every command.
