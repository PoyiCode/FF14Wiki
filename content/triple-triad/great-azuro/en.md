**Great Azuro** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Great Azuro.

“Master of knowledge beyond the comprehension of mere mortals, and wielder of arcane magicks as unfathomable as the deepest ocean... Hearken, foul beasts—the indomitable keeper of the blue has come!”
