**Huaca** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Huaca.

One of myriad beasts conjured by the great mages of the Ronkan Empire to keep watch of their holy temples. When dormant, they appear to be nothing more than ordinary statues, whose presence dominates the chambers in which they are placed. Intruders foolish enough to disturb the guardian's slumber will be forced to prove the measure of their worth, lest they be cast down into the darkest depths of the temple, never to be seen again.
