**Lala** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lala.

Encountered on Aloalo Island, the lala's wooden construction sets it apart from the stone frames of other golems. Derived from the esoteric mathematics that formed the basis of arcanima, the geometries carved into its surface enable complex and adaptive defensive measures.
