**Lunar Bahamut** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lunar Bahamut.

This manifestation of the elder primal is believed to have been called forth by Meracydian dragons held captive within one of the Telophoroi's mysterious towers. Due to the altered state of mind of those forced to perform in the summoning, its appearance differs greatly from the Bahamut that brought about the Seventh Umbral Calamity.
