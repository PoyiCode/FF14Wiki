**Shantotto the Demon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Shantotto the Demon.

A magicked doll made in the image of Vana'diel's most infamous mage. Created by Windurst's supreme mistress of sorcery herself, this mere copy of Shantotto proved a daunting opponent for Etheirys's champions. Had they faced the real deal, the outcome does not bear thinking about...
