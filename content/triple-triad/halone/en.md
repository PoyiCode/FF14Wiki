**Halone** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Halone.

Depicted as a relentless warrioress armed with a bronze greatshield, Halone is the uncontested primary deity of the Ishgardian Orthodox Church. As the mover of glaciers and goddess of war, the Fury demonstrated her propensity for battle in Euphrosyne, joyfully wielding sword and spear in her challenge against mortal adventurers.
