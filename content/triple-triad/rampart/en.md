**Rampart** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rampart.

Encountered in the undersea ruins of Alzadaal's Legacy, this floating construct seeks to discourage intruders by teleporting hostile ocean denizens into their midst. A product of Hannish alchemy and possessed of a glimmering crystal core, the rampart is more accurately classified as soulkin rather than forgekin.
