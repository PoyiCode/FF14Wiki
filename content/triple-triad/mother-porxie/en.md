**Mother Porxie** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Mother Porxie.

Baked in the heart of Matoya's Relict, this entity is able to produce entire litters of porxie offspring—a “mother” familiar mass-producing familiars for the purpose of treating the tempered. Matoya herself sculpted the mold out of clay, adding regal garments and a crown in a moment of unapologetic whimsy.
