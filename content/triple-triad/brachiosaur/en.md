**Brachiosaur** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Brachiosaur.

The discovery of this colossal creature on the isolated isles of the Diadem has perplexed naturalists, who had thought the ancient species long extinct. Possessed of an innate arcane might proportionate to its size, the brachiosaur defends itself with a meteoric rain of conjured boulders.
