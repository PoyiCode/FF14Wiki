**Magitek Colossus** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Magitek Colossus.

The colossus is an autonomous suit of magitek armor that fuses the design of the Allagan iron giant with the mechanical mobility of the Garlean vanguard. Able to switch weapons as easily as a regular soldier, the colossus is valued for its versatility on the battlefield.
