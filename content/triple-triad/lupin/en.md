**Lupin** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lupin.

This wolf-headed beast tribe has its origins on the continent of Othard. Known for their martial traditions, the Lupin became an integral part of Doman warfare after several of their clans pledged fealty to the region's warlords during their endless clash for supremacy. When the Empire seized control, however, many Lupin were conscripted into the imperial army and forced to fight abroad.
