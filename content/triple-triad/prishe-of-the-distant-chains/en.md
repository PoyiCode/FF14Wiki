**Prishe of the Distant Chains** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Prishe of the Distant Chains.

This echo was fashioned from the memories of Prishe, a young girl of Vana'diel. Her powers of recollection have been warped that she might better serve her creator's purpose, which in turn has resulted in an appearance not entirely matching that of her original form.
