**G-Warrior** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: G-Warrior.

Originally created by the Allagans as a prototype for the Ultima Weapon, this warmachina was known as the Ultima Warrior before being restored and extensively modified by Garlond Ironworks. It was later called into action in the battle with the Sapphire Weapon, where its vast range of armaments were the deciding factor in overcoming its far larger opponent. Rumors suggesting the “G” stands for “gun” remain unverified, especially considering its weapon of choice is an aethersaber.
