**Goldor** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Goldor.

In the clamoring halls of the Celestium arena did this arcane warrior clad in gold make his villainous debut. Later revealed to be yet another disguise employed by Martyn, the gilded sorcerer unleashed an assortment of spells amplified by a special device during his bout.
