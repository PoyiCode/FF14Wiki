**Statice** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Statice.

This mischievous faerie was left behind by her masters, but even after their deaths, her aether did not dissipate. After countless years of solitude, Statice's once congenial personality warped into something much more wicked. Perhaps due to a sense of kinship with other lost things, she makes ingenious use of items collected from shipwrecks and other detritus that washes ashore.
