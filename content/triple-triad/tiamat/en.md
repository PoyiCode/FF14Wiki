**Tiamat** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Tiamat.

One of the seven great wyrms sired by Midgardsormr, Father of Dragons. Following in the wake of her sibling Bahamut, Tiamat crossed to the southern continent of Meracydia where she became mother to her own expansive brood. Until this very day, she is tormented by the decision to resurrect her fallen brother as a primal in their failed resistance against the invading Allagan Empire.
