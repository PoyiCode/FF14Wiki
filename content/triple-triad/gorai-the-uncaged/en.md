**Gorai the Uncaged** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Gorai the Uncaged.

The ruin of the man once known as Gorai, head monk of the venerated Shojo Temple. An avid collector, his attachment to the many rare treasures in his possession served only to stoke the flames of his avarice—until naught of the man he was remained.
