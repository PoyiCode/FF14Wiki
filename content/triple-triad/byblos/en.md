**Byblos** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Byblos.

A forbidding presence that guards the door to the forbidden halls of the Great Gubal Library. Presumed to be a voidsent summoned from the dark realm by Sharlayan magi, it dutifully guards its post as if bound there by some dark covenant.
