**Eden's Promise** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Eden's Promise.

Formed by the fusion of Mitron and Loghrif, this mysterious entity wields Eden's power to control the elements. Its aim is to create an eternal utopia, where the two Ascians may live in peace, never to be parted.
