**Fenrir** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Fenrir.

A massive hoarhound beholden to Lady Iceheart. With its shimmering hide reminiscent of pale moonlight and a demeanor just as cold, this imposing beast was named after the legendary spirit-hound Fenrir.
