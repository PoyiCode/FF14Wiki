**Necron** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Necron.

This dreadful primal was given form by the collective fear of death experienced by the citizens of Alexandria. The intense terror felt by those suddenly forced to confront the realities of mortal existence—a truth they had long avoided by entrusting their memories of departed loved ones to the cloud—fueled the entity with a dark and inexorable power.
