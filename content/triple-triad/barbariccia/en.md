**Barbariccia** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Barbariccia.

The archfiend of wind and one of Golbez's circle of four, Barbariccia was obsessively proud of her flowing locks. Whether serving as weapons or armor, the enchanted strands of hair obeyed her every thought, and she employed them in battle with fierce aggression—a temperament which has endured since her mortal days.
