**Okuri Chochin** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Okuri Chochin.

Rokkon's chochin were once a single lantern that had acquired a spirit after many years of use—and been summarily discarded by its fearful master. It sat patiently in Gorai's collection for a time, but when wickedness pervaded the temple, it divided itself into a multitude of mononoke, the better to devour men's souls as recompense for its abandonment.
