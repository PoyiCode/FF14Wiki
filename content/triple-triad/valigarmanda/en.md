**Valigarmanda** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Valigarmanda.

Having nurtured immense strength over its unnaturally long lifespan, the winged serpent known also as the “Skyruin” ruled uncontested over the lands of Tural for centuries. It was sealed in ice some eight decades earlier by Gulool Ja Ja, only to be freed again during the recent rite of succession.
