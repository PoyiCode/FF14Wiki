**Phoebad** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Phoebad.

Phoebads are a type of golem that occur naturally in areas rich in salt crystals, such as Loch Seld and other similar bodies of water. In folklore, phoebads are said to have once been people who defied the revelations of Rhalgr, and were transformed as punishment for their disobedience.
