**Black Cat** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Black Cat.

Wielding the feral soul of the dread feline Cath Palug, this razor-sharp clawed rookie of the Arcadion tears across the ring—and through her foes—with astonishing agility.
