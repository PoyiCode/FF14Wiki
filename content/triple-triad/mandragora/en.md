**Mandragora** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Mandragora.

The enigmatic mandragoras were first observed in the aftermath of the Seventh Umbral Era. It is not known whence they came, or if they even existed prior to the Calamity. Preliminary studies appear to suggest that the diminutive seedkin are sentient and capable of an advanced form of nonverbal communication, but further research is required to confirm these findings. The rest is mere speculation.
