**Lugus** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lugus.

A familiar of flame birthed by the recluse mage Beq Lugg, whose original intent was to more efficiently create porxies. An experiment gone awry, however, resulted in the flames of a fae lantern gaining sentience. Realizing that such a creature would be far better suited to protecting their domain, further research was conducted, leading to the creation of Lugus.
