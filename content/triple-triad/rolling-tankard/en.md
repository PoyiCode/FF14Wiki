**Rolling Tankard** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rolling Tankard.

The latest creation of Watts's Anvil, built to accommodate caravans seeking to transport goods throughout Norvrandt without being assailed by sin eaters. Furnished with ample storage space and an automaton pilot, one need only input a destination and enjoy the ride.
