**Thaliak** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Thaliak.

Thaliak, ruler of rivers and wisdom and god of knowledge, is most often depicted as a reserved scholar holding an ashen staff. The citizens of Sharlayan voted to adopt Thaliak as their guardian deity, and his appearance in Thaleia is said to closely resemble the grand statue they raised in his name.
