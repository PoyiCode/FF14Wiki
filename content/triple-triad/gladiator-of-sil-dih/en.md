**Gladiator of Sil'dih** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Gladiator of Sil'dih.

Discovered in the ruins of Sil'dih, this warrior statue bears a resemblance to the guardians of Azeyma's temple. The similarity may be attributed to the use of Belah'dian technology in their construction, though this particular unit appears to have been designed to excel at swordfighting.
