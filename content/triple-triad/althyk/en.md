**Althyk** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Althyk.

Althyk is the god of time and the surveyor of change and space. He commands the element of earth, and is most often depicted as an austere emperor wielding a mythril greataxe. Said to be the first of the Twelve, his presence in Euphrosyne is one characterized by quiet authority, though he seems much softened in dealings with his younger sister, Nymeia.
