**Enenra** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Enenra.

This tsukumogami arose from a pipe said to grant its owner joy or sorrow according to its whims—though the fates of those that once possessed it seem curiously amenable to either interpretation, depending on who tells the tale. Perhaps that is why it takes the form of smoke, shrouding that which mortal eyes are so desperate to see laid bare.
