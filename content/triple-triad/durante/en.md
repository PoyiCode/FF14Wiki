**Durante** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Durante.

Glimpsed within a fragment of Golbez's past, this memoriate swordsman strove to save the world at the side of his black-armored companion. Yet it was not to be. In a final act of vengeance, a fallen enemy corrupted Golbez, and Durante was forced to raise a blade against his former friend.
