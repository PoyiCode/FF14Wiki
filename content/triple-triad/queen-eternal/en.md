**Queen Eternal** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Queen Eternal.

Having resolved to plunder aether from other worlds, Sphene shed her conscience by erasing her memories and arose as this entity within the Meso Terminal. It wields the absolute authority of Alexandria's eternal queen, and exists solely to sustain the Endless.
