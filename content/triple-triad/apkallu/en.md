**Apkallu** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Apkallu.

These large, flightless seabirds dwell on the coastlines of Vylbrand. Though apkallu move on land with an awkward waddle, they use their flipper-like wings to glide through the water at impressive speeds. Able to adjust their stomach acids at will, the green-feathered cloudkin store fish in their bellies for feeding their young, but have also been known to spit caustic fluids at predators.
