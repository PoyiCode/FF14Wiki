**Wivre** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Wivre.

A ferocious and powerful scalekin native to Tural. The Mamool Ja have succeeded in their domestication, and employ the creatures as beasts of burden in daily life and living battering rams in war. Those specimens seen in Eorzea in recent years were brought across the seas by Mamool Ja mercenaries.
