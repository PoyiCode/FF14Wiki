**Koja** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Koja.

A golem mainly constructed for the purpose of serving onmyoji and geomancers, the bulbous form of a koja is fashioned from sticky mud and imbued with the spirit of a shikigami. The features common to all koja are believed to be modeled after the original creator—a eunuch from ancient times—but no portrait has ever been found to substantiate this claim.
