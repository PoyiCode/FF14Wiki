**Troll** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Troll.

An Aerslaent fiend of towering height, a troll's upper body is covered in bristly hairs, while its lower half is exposed skin with the hardened appearance of pinecone. To the Roegadyn of its home island, this beast is seen as a rite of passage, for those who can defeat one are granted license to board the longships.
