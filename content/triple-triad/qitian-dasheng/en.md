**Qitian Dasheng** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Qitian Dasheng.

As with others of its ilk, this simian auspice gained divine might after enduring a millennium of life. Qitian Dasheng, whose name means “Great Sage, Heaven's Equal,” dwelled in the Swallow's Compass to prevent his master's creation—the tamate-bako of geomancy—from falling into unscrupulous hands.
