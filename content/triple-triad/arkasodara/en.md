**Arkasodara** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Arkasodara.

A faction of Matanga whose ancestors were indigenous to Thavnair. Following the appearance of the Au Ra, they joined forces with these new arrivals to drive the rival Gajasura tribe from their lands. Later, Hyuran settlers would come to the island's shores, and together did these disparate peoples establish the city-state of Radz-at-Han.
