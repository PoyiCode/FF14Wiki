**Phlegethon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Phlegethon.

Phlegethon was a hero of the rebellion that sought to oppose the rule of Allag in the twilight of the Third Astral Era. After his capture by the technologist Amon, the valiant general was subjected to hideous experiments, his body altered and mind broken, fated to become an eternal guardian of the Crystal Tower.
