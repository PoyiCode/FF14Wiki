**Athena** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Athena.

Pandæmonium's first chief keyward, Athena used a man named Claudien as a vessel to house her memories, traversing eons and coming within an ilm of godhood. Luckily for the whole star, her ambitions were cut short before they could be realized.
