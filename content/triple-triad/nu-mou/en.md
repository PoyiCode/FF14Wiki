**Nu Mou** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Nu Mou.

A fae race that inhabits the faerie kingdom of Il Mheg. Noted for their warm disposition and welcoming attitude towards the mortal races, they enjoyed close relations with the Kingdom of Voeburt in the days before the Flood. With fair exchange as their foremost principle, they are willing to barter for a wide range of goods and services.
