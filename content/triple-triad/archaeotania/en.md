**Archaeotania** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Archaeotania.

This massive and monstrous beast that arose from the depths of the Tempest is said to bear a striking resemblance to one of the calamitous fiends that brought death and destruction to the civilization of the ancients. One rumor has it that it was recreated by the Ascian Emet-Selch, that people of this day might know the terror that he and his brethren suffered, but the truth remains shrouded in mystery.
