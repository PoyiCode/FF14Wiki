**Therion** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Therion.

As Amaurot stood upon the precipice of oblivion, Therion was one of many harbingers who would see the world ravaged by flames. Knowing the Amaurotines themselves gave birth to these unspeakable horrors perhaps made them all the more terrible as they ushered in the Final Days.
