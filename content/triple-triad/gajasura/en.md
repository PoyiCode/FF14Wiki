**Gajasura** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Gajasura.

This Matanga tribe was driven from its indigenous home of Thavnair when it refused to accept the peace made between the Arkasodara and newly immigrated Au Ra. Said to spread discord wherever they go, the Gajasura finally resettled on the Azim Steppe, where their loathing for the Au Ra festers to this day.
