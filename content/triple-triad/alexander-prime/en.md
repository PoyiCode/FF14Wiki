**Alexander Prime** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Alexander Prime.

Alexander Prime served as the last line of defense for the mechanical primal's innermost core. As an integral element of the core's central control system, Alexander Prime exhibited a limited ability to manipulate temporal reality, and sought to smite intruders with its divine judgment.
