**Yiazmat** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Yiazmat.

Little remains of former Dalmascan fusilier captain Ba'Gamnan, now twisted by the Duma into a soulless Lucavi who survives solely by drinking deep of the fallen Bangaa's deepest desires.
