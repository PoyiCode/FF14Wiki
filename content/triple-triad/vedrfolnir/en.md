**Vedrfolnir** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Vedrfolnir.

Sired by the great wyrm Hraesvelgr, Vedrfolnir's fiercely aggressive demeanor sets him apart from his tranquil broodmates. This mighty dragon revels in the glory of combat, and has fostered a deadly rivalry with Darkscale, a general in Nidhogg's Horde.
