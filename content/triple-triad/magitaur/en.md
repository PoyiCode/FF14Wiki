**Magitaur** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Magitaur.

This fearsome voidsent encountered at the summit of the Tower of Blood is believed to have been called into the corporeal plane via advanced Mhachi summoning techniques. It appears the architect of the Occult Crescent had forged a covenant with the being, and granted it special weapons with which to defend the tower.
