**Shadowbringers Warrior of Light** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Shadowbringers Warrior of Light.

A being summoned into existence through the peoples' hopes and prayers for salvation. One can only speculate as to what era, what far-flung world, inspired its otherwise antiquated visage. Perhaps it is the culmination of ideals to which all heroes aspire.
