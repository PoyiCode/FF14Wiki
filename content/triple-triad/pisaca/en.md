**Pisaca** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Pisaca.

A vicious Thavnairian scalekin with plating as strong as steel, claws as sharp as swords, and a tongue as long and lacerating as a whip. Its savagery is such that it rarely hesitates to attack even armed travelers. Little wonder, then, that local legends cast it as a fierce demon.
