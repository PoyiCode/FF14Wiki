**Ixal** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ixal.

This aggressive mountain tribe claim the wind-blasted canyons of Xelphatol as their domain. Though their race has long since lost the capacity for flight, the feathered beastmen are fervently proud of their vestigial wings. The Ixal seek to reestablish their presence in the Black Shroud that they might once again fell trees in worship of their fickle deity—an ambition which places them at odds with the people of Gridania.
