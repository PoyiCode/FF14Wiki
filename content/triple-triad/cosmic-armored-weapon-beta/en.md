**Cosmic Armored Weapon Beta** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Cosmic Armored Weapon Beta.

This machina deployed on Auxesia is based on the imperial armored weapon, with modifications made to enable effective fertilizer transport and dispersal. Its arachnoid legs lend it exceptional stability and agility, and it can climb the trunks of great trees with ease.
