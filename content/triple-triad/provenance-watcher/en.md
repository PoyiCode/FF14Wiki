**Provenance Watcher** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Provenance Watcher.

This crystalline monstrosity is known to dwell in the watery reaches of Eureka Hydatos. Although it takes the shape of a greater dragon, the creature is believed to have simply mimicked a wyrm-like form, and is, in fact, closer in nature to the luminare—an arcane entity spawned by a concentration of ambient aether.
