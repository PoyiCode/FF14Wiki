**Lindwurm** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lindwurm.

The dread being that arose to challenge the unenhanced champion of the Arcadion. Its true identity was none other than the president of Ascension Arcadia, who had infused himself with the soul of the legendary serpent, thereby acquiring its fearsome form and phenomenal regenerative power.
