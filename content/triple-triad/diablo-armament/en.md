**Diablo Armament** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Diablo Armament.

Hidden deep within the ruins below Zadnor, it was recently unearthed by Menenius of the IVth Imperial Legion. Evidence indicates that it is a voidsent which has undergone aetherochemical enhancement, in order to participate in the subjugation of the southern continent.
