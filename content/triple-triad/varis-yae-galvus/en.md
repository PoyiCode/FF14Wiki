**Varis yae Galvus** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Varis yae Galvus.

Before being crowned emperor, Varis yae Galvus served as high legatus, commanding the vast armies of the Garlean Empire. It was long believed he had a hand in the accident that laid waste to Bozja Citadel, but this was no more than a figment of Cid Garlond's imagination—a means to cope with the truth of his traumatic past.
