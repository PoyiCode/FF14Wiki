**Ornamental Leafman** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ornamental Leafman.

Encountered along Pilgrim's Traverse in the environs of Il Mheg, this botanical behemoth is thought to be an unfortunate mortal transformed by fae magicks. Whether its extreme belligerence is a vestige of its former self or the result of transfiguration remains unclear.
