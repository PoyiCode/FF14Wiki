**Lone Swordmaster** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lone Swordmaster.

Expert practitioners of the Unyielding Blade, the traditional Corvosi style of swordplay, are formally known as swordmasters. It is said the last man to hold the title was taken captive following a failed rebellion against the Garlean Empire and ultimately slain by Zenos yae Galvus.
