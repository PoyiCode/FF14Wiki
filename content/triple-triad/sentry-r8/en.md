**Sentry R8** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Sentry R8.

The pride of Alexandria's mechanical army, the sentry unit comes in several variations, including the gun-wielding G-type and the sword-swinging S-type. The R-type is designed to operate military-grade airborne vehicles with deadly efficiency.
