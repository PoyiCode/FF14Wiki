**Mica the Magical Mu** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Mica the Magical Mu.

This mascot of Living Memory was created for the purpose of bringing smiles as brilliant as the mica for which it was named. Due to a lack of regular maintenance, however, its programmed behavior has become increasingly erratic.
