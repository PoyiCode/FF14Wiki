**Eald'narche** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Eald'narche.

This echo was formed from the memories of Eald'narche, older brother of Kam'lanaut, who governed Jeuno alongside him in Vana'diel. The eldest prince of the ancient Zilart, he remains undeterred by the passage of ages in his quest to open the Gates of Paradise.
