**Emerald Weapon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Emerald Weapon.

Constructed by the VIIth Legion, this Ultima Weapon prototype is infused with the aether of an unknown primal summoned by the mountain tribes of central Ilsabard. The warmachina boasts simultaneous control over multiple free-floating manipulators and is said to employ combat data from Gaius van Baelsar as a basis for its attack parameters.
