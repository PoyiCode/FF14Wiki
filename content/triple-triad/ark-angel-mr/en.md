**Ark Angel MR** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ark Angel MR.

An echo formed from the memories of Vana'diel's Crystal Warriors. Resembling a Mithra, this entity is said to embody that race's deep-seated envy.
