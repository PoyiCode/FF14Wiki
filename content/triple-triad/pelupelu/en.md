**Pelupelu** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Pelupelu.

Known first and foremost as peddlers, this people of small stature hail from the mountainous region of Urqopacha. They can be seen traveling with their trusty alpacas across the length and breadth of Tural, their characteristic beak-like masks designed to resemble the legendary tural vidraal, Valigarmanda.
