**Interstellar Dhalmel** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Interstellar Dhalmel.

This dhalmel was a subject in adaptability testing conducted for the Cosmic Exploration Initiative. Owing to the potential presence of harmful gases on other planets, it has been fitted with custom protective equipment, which includes a full-face helmet.
