**Doomtrain** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Doomtrain.

This armored vehicle built by the nation of Lindblum in the final years of the Storm Surge operates autonomously by way of an arcane entity housed in the engine. Designed to transport war-beasts to battle, it ended up speeding across the continent for centuries with a horde of fiends in its wake.
