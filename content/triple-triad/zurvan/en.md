**Zurvan** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Zurvan.

One of the three eikons who comprise the Warring Triad. Worshipped by a centaur-like race on Meracydia, Zurvan was known as the deity of eternity, or “He who standeth above all other gods.” Brought into being by his followers as a symbol of overwhelming victory, this entity's frightful appearance and merciless appetite for justice earned him the title of the “Demon.”
