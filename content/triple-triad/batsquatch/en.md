**Batsquatch** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Batsquatch.

A species of giant bat relocated to the Qitana Ravel to deter would-be thieves from trespassing in the ruins. In the searing Light of the Flood, their already limited eyesight was lost. By contrast, their already exceptional hearing and use of echolocation improved dramatically.
