**Gogo, Master of Mimicry** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Gogo, Master of Mimicry.

This extraordinary individual made a name for himself as “Golden Goliath,” the reigning champion of Kugane's Fukumen Fighting Festival. Gogo has developed the singular ability to instantaneously read his opponents' moves down to the aetheric level, allowing him to mimic almost any technique to full and devastating effect.
