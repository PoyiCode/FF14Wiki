**Bismarck** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Bismarck.

Long thought to be little more than myth, the Lord of the Mists was summoned forth and given form by the fervent prayers of the Vanu Vanu, who worship him as the god that led their ancestors to their island home. With a flood-blast of water from his great blowhole, the great white whale purges the skies of those who would do his followers harm.
