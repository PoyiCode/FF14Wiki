**Rukhkh** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rukhkh.

This chilling cloudkin is an antagonizing force in *The Merchant's Tale*, a fable of Corvosi origin. Though details vary by region and generation, all versions depict a predator that cloaks itself in cold fog, snatching up seafaring ships in its great talons and dropping them to their doom.
