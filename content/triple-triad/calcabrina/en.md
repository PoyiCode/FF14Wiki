**Calcabrina** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Calcabrina.

This doll-like construct was created by the Sharlayans to protect the sanctity of the Antitower. Steeped in the energies of generations of mages, the powerful magicks instilled in this guardian have worked eerie transmutations upon its form.
