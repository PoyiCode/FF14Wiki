**Titania** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Titania.

King and ruler of the faeries, Titania once defeated a Lightwarden that threatened Il Mheg, only to be corrupted by the aether that was subsequently released. For long years thence, the fallen monarch was kept imprisoned within Lyhe Ghiah.
