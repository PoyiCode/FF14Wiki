**Pazuzu** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Pazuzu.

Explorers of Eureka Anemos claim that when the wind howls across that forbidden land, it is the sound of the dead heralding the advent of high voidsent and lord of the gales, Pazuzu. Considering what little is known of the Isle of Val and its logic-defying laws of nature, those claims could bear more truth than fiction.
