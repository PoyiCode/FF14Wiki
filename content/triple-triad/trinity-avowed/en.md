**Trinity Avowed** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Trinity Avowed.

An amalgamation of three members of Gunnhildr's Blades—Xeven the Vengeful, Isolde the Eagle-eye, and Stanik the Unwavering. Originally enthralled at the behest of Misija, the trio's physical forms were eventually meshed into a singular, abhorrent entity within which now resides the sum of their arcane and martial expertise.
