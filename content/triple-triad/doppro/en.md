**Doppro** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Doppro.

One of the three Mamool Ja clans, the Doppro are identifiable by their distinct speckled scales. Despite being far fewer in number than the Hoobigo and Boonewa, they are by no means considered lesser in status; on the contrary, they are highly regarded for their mastery in the handling of wild beasts such as the wivre.
