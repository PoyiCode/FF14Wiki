**Menphina** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Menphina.

The keeper of the twin moons and the goddess of love. Menphina commands the element of ice, and is often depicted as a maid carrying a round skillet. The large hound which appeared at her side in the phantom realm was a manifestation of Dalamud—the lesser moon known in legend as Menphina's loyal hound.
