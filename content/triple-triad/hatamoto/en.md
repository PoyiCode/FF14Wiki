**Hatamoto** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Hatamoto.

The spirit of an armored warrior who died in battle. The hatamoto are a class of soldier in Hingashi who often fought as personal guards for the nobility during the Age of Blood. Great numbers of these loyal vassals perished on the battlefield, and their battered, armor-clad forms are sometimes still spotted wandering the land in search of elusive victory.
