**Nald** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Nald.

Overseer of the underworld, god of commerce, and guardian deity of Ul'dah, Nald is conventionally depicted alongside Thal as either his twin or a single god of dual aspect. In Aglaia, he appraises the value of pilgrims' souls as any good trader would.
