**Guidance Node** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Guidance Node.

An automated machina designed to serve as a guide to Azys Lla's myriad facilities. Its operating system, an amalgamation of both military and civilian protocols, was the first of its kind, but the attempt to fuse contradictory rule sets resulted in a general purpose protocol containing 2 to the power of 16 system glitches.
