**Kraken** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Kraken.

The terror that sank a thousand ships, this legendary sea monster once guarded the secret cove of Mistbeard, the pirate king. Following its defeat at the hands of treasure-seeking adventurers, however, the kraken fled into the open sea, where it encountered a kindred spirit in Captain Madison.
