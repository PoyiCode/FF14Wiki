**Shinryu (Vana'diel)** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Shinryu (Vana'diel).

Born from the memories of an entity said to be terror and dread made flesh. In another time, in another world, this mighty deity was said to be created when the Keeper of the Apocalypse absorbed a being possessed of great power. It was even said to have brought low the hero thought destined to save the world from ruin.
