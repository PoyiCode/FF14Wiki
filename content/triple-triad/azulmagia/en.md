**Azulmagia** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Azulmagia.

This combatant was introduced to the Celestium's roster of villains as the “dread warlock who transcends dimensions of time and space.” Whastrach—as he is known outside the arena—was once a researcher affiliated with the Arcanists' Guild, and mastered the secrets of blue magic during the time he spent studying the Whalaqee tribe.
