**Ark Angel HM** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ark Angel HM.

An echo formed from the memories of Vana'diel's Crystal Warriors. Resembling a Hume, this entity is said to embody that race's deep-seated apathy.
