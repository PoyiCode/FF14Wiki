**Promathia** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Promathia.

This echo was formed from the memories of the Twilight God Promathia. He is the darkness to the Dawn Goddess Altana's radiance, an empty-hearted incarnation born of arrogance, envy, cowardice, apathy, and rage.
