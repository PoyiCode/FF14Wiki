**Evil Weapon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Evil Weapon.

The evil weapon is a curious form of arcane entity, easily recognized by the dancing sword which floats above its squat, monstrous body. Contrary to appearances, however, the enchanted sword itself holds the mind, whilst the body acts as a puppet. Such beings were once prized as servants in the nation of Nabaath Areng, and thus it is not uncommon to encounter them roaming the badlands, bereft of master and purpose.
