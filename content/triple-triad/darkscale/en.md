**Darkscale** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Darkscale.

A descendant of Nidhogg, this powerful dragon boasts command over a significant army of underlings within the Horde. Darkscale is highly contemptuous of Hraesvelgr and his brood's refusal to join the Dragonsong War—an animosity that has festered into bitter rivalry with the white dragon, Vedrfolnir.
