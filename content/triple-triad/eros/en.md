**Eros** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Eros.

The Lightwarden of the Rak'tika Greatwood. A chimerical monstrosity, Eros was thought to be the amalgamation of several unfortunate creatures that were caught up and fused together by the Flood of Light. The meaning of its name—“romantic love”—is a mocking play upon the intimate and uncontrolled embrace which resulted in its present appearance.
