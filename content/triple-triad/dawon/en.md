**Dawon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Dawon.

Were one foolish enough to seek such a creature as this for their own, they would journey to the central mountain ranges of Ilsabard. It is said to challenge Dawon is to court death itself—yet Lyon rem Helsos not only captured the ill-tempered monstrosity, but tamed it as well, earning himself the moniker “the Beast King.”
