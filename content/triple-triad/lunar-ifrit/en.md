**Lunar Ifrit** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lunar Ifrit.

This twisted fiend is believed to have been forcibly summoned by Amalj'aa held captive in one of the Telophoroi's towers. While similar in many ways to the Lord of the Inferno, he lacks the capacity to temper those in his vicinity.
