**Ryoqor Terteh** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ryoqor Terteh.

Ryoqor Terteh is often spotted frolicking on the frigid slopes of Worqor Zormor with its lesser kin. In the Yok Huy tongue, its name means “talkative snow spirit from the moon.” True to the description, it seems to delight in inviting travelers to play in its snowy games.
