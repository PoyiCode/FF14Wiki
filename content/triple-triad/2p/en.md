**2P** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: 2P.

A woman encountered in the excavation tunnels who claims to be an android. She leads the white-clad androids in a battle against those in black, though it remains to be seen to what end.
