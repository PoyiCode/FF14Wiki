**Ark Angel EV** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ark Angel EV.

An echo formed from the memories of Vana'diel's Crystal Warriors. Resembling an Elvaan, this entity is said to embody that race's deep-seated arrogance.
