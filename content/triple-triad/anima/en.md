**Anima** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Anima.

A primal housed within the Tower of Babil, Anima was brought forth with the corpse of Varis zos Galvus and granted its unearthly power through the patriotism and fervor of the Empire's citizens. It sat at the center of the Telophoroi's web, siphoning rivers of aether through the spires built in every far-flung corner of the world.
