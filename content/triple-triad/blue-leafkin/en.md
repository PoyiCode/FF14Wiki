**Blue Leafkin** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Blue Leafkin.

A variety of seedkin whose head and body are cloaked in leaves. Those that inhabit Yak T'el have taken on a luminous blue hue owing to the influence of the meteorites scattered throughout the forest. The local Mamool Ja hold the creatures to be their kind who were cursed, and go to great lengths to avoid them lest they be likewise afflicted.
