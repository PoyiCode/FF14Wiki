**Shishio** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Shishio.

A mythical beast feared in Hingashi since the days of yore, this creature figures in over one hundred legends. While some believe it to be the king of the nue, others insist it is a manifestation of fear itself, born from the quavering hearts of men who have heard the cries that reverberate amongst the mountains of Shishu at night.
