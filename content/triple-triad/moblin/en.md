**Moblin** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Moblin.

Making its home in the dense forests of Kozama'uka, this eccentric clan of expert miners and gatherers shares a common ancestry with the goblins. They make their living by providing contracted artisans—their “potsworn”—with ample crafting materials, and ensuring a perfect working environment for the creation of profitable masterpieces.
