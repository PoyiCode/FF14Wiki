**Drowsie** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Drowsie.

This odd specimen of the uolon—a creature closely related to the troll—purposefully swallows slimes to “raise” them in its belly. Although the loss of nutrients results in frequent bouts of drowsiness, it allows the beast to spit up its ingested guests as battle reinforcements should it ever feel threatened.
