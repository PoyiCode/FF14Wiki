**Genie of the Lamp** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Genie of the Lamp.

Full many Corvosi legends are centered on mystical artifacts, the magic lamp foremost among them. Give it a firm rub, the story goes, to draw forth a faerie with the ability to grant wishes.
