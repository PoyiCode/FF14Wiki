**Waukkeon** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Waukkeon.

This giant sanuwa nests on Neverreap, an island sacred to the Vanu Vanu. The scales of the waukkeon glisten with liquid vibrancy, as if the life-giving rains that fall upon the floating isles were trapped within. If such a treasure could be pried from its hide, one might fashion a mask that granted the authority of a furious storm cloud.
