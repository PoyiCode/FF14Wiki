**Valia Pira** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Valia Pira.

Once deployed in the castle of Alexandria as a defense mechanism, the Valia Pira was reactivated by Calyx to test the capabilities of the Warrior of Light. It executed unpredictable attack patterns by manifesting orbs of aetherial power to energize its roaming sub-units.
