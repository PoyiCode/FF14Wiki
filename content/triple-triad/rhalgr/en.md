**Rhalgr** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rhalgr.

Breaker of worlds and god of destruction, Rhalgr commands the power of lightning and is often depicted as a magus carrying a staff of bronze. The mighty physical form he takes in Aglaia is believed to reflect the visualizations of his most pious monks, the Fist of Rhalgr.
