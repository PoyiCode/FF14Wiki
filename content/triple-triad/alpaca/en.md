**Alpaca** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Alpaca.

These four-legged beasts are native to Tural's mountains. Possessing the conflicting traits of both curiosity and cowardice, they are famous for spitting foul-smelling globs of expectoration at those who startle them. The wild alpacas which dwell in the valleys are said to be a particularly wary breed.
