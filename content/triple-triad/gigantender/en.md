**Gigantender** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Gigantender.

Primarily found in Amh Araeng and other arid desert regions, the gigantender is distinguished from the common sabotender by its unusual size and crown of multiple branches. Their fruit is said to be deliciously sweet but difficult to consume due to the large number of seeds they hold.
