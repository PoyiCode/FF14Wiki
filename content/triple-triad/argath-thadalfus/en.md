**Argath Thadalfus** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Argath Thadalfus.

A minor noble long deceased, Argath is resurrected and granted unimaginable power by the Duma—a rare crystal thought to be naught more than legend. Drunk on this newfound strength, Argath takes on the form of a two-faced god, terrorizing all those who would seek an audience deep within the sealed ruins of the Royal City of Lesalia.
