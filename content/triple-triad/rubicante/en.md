**Rubicante** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Rubicante.

The archfiend of fire, and one of Golbez's circle of four. This intimidating warrior would pursue his goals with zealous abandon, conjuring hellish flames from the ashes of his own suffering in a bid to vanquish his foes.
