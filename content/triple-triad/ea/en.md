**Ea** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Ea.

An ancient race of sentient life-forms which Meteion reanimated within Ultima Thule. So advanced was their civilization, they were able to unlock the secret to immortality. However, their discovery of the universe's inevitable destruction saw them sink into a despair so unending and dark, they prayed for deliverance through extinction.
