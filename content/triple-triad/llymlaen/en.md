**Llymlaen** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Llymlaen.

Llymlaen, watcher of the seas and goddess of navigation, is depicted as a strong fisherwoman wielding a long-bladed harpoon. Described in myth as Oschon's lover, the tale of the bathing Llymlaen hurling a sword at him for peeping is generally agreed to be the later fabrication of whimsical bards.
