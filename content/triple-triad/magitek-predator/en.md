**Magitek Predator** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Magitek Predator.

The most recent model in the Empire's line of piloted magitek armor. Though based on the reaper design, the predator has been outfitted with bladed arms to improve its close-quarter combat capabilities and also features simplified armor plating for enhanced mobility.
