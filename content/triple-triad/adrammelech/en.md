**Adrammelech** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Adrammelech.

This entity is bound to the will of imperial pilus prior Albeleo, who is said to have effected the summoning with an auracite stone recovered from the ruins of Dalmasca. Readings of ancient inscriptions found the creature described simply as “the Wroth.”
