**Amaro** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Amaro.

These flying beasts are the primary form of transportation in the region of Norvrandt. Once similar to the cama, the creatures now known as “amaro” are the result of a magically enhanced selective breeding process conducted in ages past by Ronkan sorcerers. Although the techniques employed are long since lost, the old bloodline has yet been known to quicken in individual amaro, manifesting in the form of superior intellect.
