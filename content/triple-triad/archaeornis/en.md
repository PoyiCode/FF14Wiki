**Archaeornis** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Archaeornis.

These large, feathered beasts are primarily found in the northern reaches of Aldenard, and are often seen under the sway of the more powerful dragons. Long thought to belong to the cloudkin family, recent studies claim that the archaeornis are, in fact, descended from an ancient species of scalekin.
