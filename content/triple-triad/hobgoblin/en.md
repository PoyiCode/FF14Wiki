**Hobgoblin** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Hobgoblin.

These brutish folk have long plagued the region of Norvrandt. Reasonably intelligent and capable of speech and toolcraft, they are nonetheless devoid of anything resembling a moral compass, and primarily occupy themselves with thieving and trickery. Amongst the dwarves, few insults cut as sharp as having one's mother likened to a hobgoblin.
