**Crawler** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Crawler.

This corpulent vilekin infests the Dravanian hinterlands, its distinctive black and yellow coloring warning potential predators of its toxic nature. Curious gourmands beware: consuming a crawler's flesh without the proper preparation invariably results in excruciating abdominal pain.
