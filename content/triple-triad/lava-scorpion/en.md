**Lava Scorpion** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Lava Scorpion.

A creature of living lava, this hulking nightmare lurked in the pools of magma hidden within the heart of Sohm Al. When the dragons were no longer present to keep the scorpion in check, the ancient menace resurfaced and began agitating the mountain's volcanic core with its wild thrashing.
