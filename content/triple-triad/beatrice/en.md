**Beatrice** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Beatrice.

An entity encountered in the Thirteenth, this high-ranking voidsent was mistress of a domain built on the memories of a once-existing castle. She is said to have led the cabal of eight priestesses who governed Troia, a nation since lost to the Darkness.
