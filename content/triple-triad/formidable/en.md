**Formidable** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Formidable.

The Goggs employed every engineering trick in the book to complete the construction of this gigantic automaton. Seeking only to outdo the works of their hated rivals, the Tholls, they focused on sheer size to the exclusion of all else—an oversight which resulted in the creation of a massive, unstable machine that no dwarf could control.
