**Erichthonios** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Erichthonios.

The harrowing form taken by Erichthonios—warder of the condemned—when he set upon Themis and the Warrior of Light as they attempted to breach Pandæmonium in search of the truth. He is purportedly skilled in shackling magicks...to the exclusion of much else.
