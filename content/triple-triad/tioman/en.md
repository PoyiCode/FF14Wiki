**Tioman** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Tioman.

A mighty and vengeful dragon who makes her lair in the ghostly peak of Sohm Al. Consort of Nidhogg, one of the seven great wyrms, Tioman is known as the dark dragon among the Ishgardian people by virtue of her scales, which are said to be darker than a starless night.
