**Darya the Sea-maid** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Darya the Sea-maid.

Some say that Corvos harbors a hidden community of maritime folk half-covered with scales. Lovers of the sea are welcomed warmly, but woe betide any who dare to pollute it. This depiction also features in certain editions of *The Merchant's Tale*.
