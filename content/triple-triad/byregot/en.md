**Byregot** is a Triple Triad card — collected and played at the Gold Saucer, a pastime residents love. In-game name: Byregot.

The god of architecture and industry, Byregot commands the element of lightning, and is most often depicted as an ardent smith wielding a two-headed hammer. The muscular form he takes in Aglaia is a testament to faith in physical strength, a conviction common amongst the builders and laborers who worship him.
